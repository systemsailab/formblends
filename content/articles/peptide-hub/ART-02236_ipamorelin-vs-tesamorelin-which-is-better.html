Ipamorelin vs Tesamorelin: Which Is Better?

Quick Answer: Ipamorelin vs Tesamorelin compares a two growth hormone secretagogues working through different receptor families. Ipamorelin activates the ghrelin receptor (GHS-R) to trigger GH pulses. Tesamorelin activates the GHRH receptor to amplify GH release. They can be combined for synergistic GH stimulation, similar to how Ipamorelin pairs with CJC-1295 .

Can You Use Both?

Yes. Ipamorelin and Tesamorelin work through independent mechanisms and do not interfere with each other. They can be combined when your health goals span both selective gh release and gh stimulation. Your physician can design a protocol that incorporates both compounds with appropriate timing and dosing.
