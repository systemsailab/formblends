Sermorelin vs MOTS-c: Which Is Better?

Quick Answer: Sermorelin vs MOTS-c compares a growth hormone peptide with a mitochondrial metabolic peptide. Sermorelin stimulates GH for body composition, sleep, and recovery. MOTS-c activates AMPK pathways to improve insulin sensitivity, glucose metabolism, and exercise performance. Both support metabolic health but through different pathways: hormonal vs mitochondrial {{CITE:Sermorelin and MOTS-c serve different therapeutic purposes}}.

Can You Use Both?

Yes. Sermorelin and MOTS-c work through independent mechanisms and do not interfere with each other. They can be combined when your health goals span both stimulate natural gh production and metabolic optimization. Your physician can design a protocol that incorporates both compounds with appropriate timing and dosing.
