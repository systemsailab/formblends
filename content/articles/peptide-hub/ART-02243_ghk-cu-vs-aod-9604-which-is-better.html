GHK-Cu vs AOD-9604: Which Is Better?

Quick Answer: GHK-Cu vs AOD-9604 compares a tissue remodeling peptide with a fat-loss peptide. GHK-Cu is used for collagen stimulation, skin rejuvenation. AOD-9604 is used for lipolysis stimulation. These compounds serve different purposes and your choice depends on your primary health goal. They can often be used together since they work through independent mechanisms .

Can You Use Both?

Yes. GHK-Cu and AOD-9604 work through independent mechanisms and do not interfere with each other. They can be combined when your health goals span both collagen stimulation and lipolysis stimulation. Your physician can design a protocol that incorporates both compounds with appropriate timing and dosing.
