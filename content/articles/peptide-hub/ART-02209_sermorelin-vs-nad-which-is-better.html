Sermorelin vs NAD+: Which Is Better?

Quick Answer: Sermorelin vs NAD+ compares a growth hormone peptide with a cellular energy coenzyme. Sermorelin stimulates GH release for body composition, sleep, and recovery. NAD+ supports mitochondrial energy production, DNA repair, and sirtuin-mediated longevity pathways. Both are anti-aging compounds but work through completely different mechanisms: hormonal optimization vs cellular energy restoration .

Can You Use Both?

Yes. Sermorelin and NAD+ work through independent mechanisms and do not interfere with each other. They can be combined when your health goals span both stimulate natural gh production and cellular energy. Your physician can design a protocol that incorporates both compounds with appropriate timing and dosing.
