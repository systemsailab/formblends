CJC-1295 vs NAD+: Which Is Better?

Quick Answer: CJC-1295 vs NAD+ compares a growth hormone peptide with a cellular energy coenzyme. CJC-1295 stimulates GH release for body composition, sleep, and recovery. NAD+ supports mitochondrial function, DNA repair, and sirtuin activation. Both contribute to anti-aging but through different mechanisms: hormonal optimization vs cellular energy and DNA maintenance. Can You Use Both?

Yes. CJC-1295 and NAD+ work through independent mechanisms and do not interfere with each other. They can be combined when your health goals span both extended gh stimulation and cellular energy. Your physician can design a protocol that incorporates both compounds with appropriate timing and dosing.
