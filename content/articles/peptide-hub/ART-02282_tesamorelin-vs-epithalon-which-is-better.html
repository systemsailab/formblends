Tesamorelin vs Epithalon: Which Is Better?

Quick Answer: Tesamorelin vs Epithalon compares a growth hormone secretagogue with a telomerase-activating peptide. Tesamorelin is used for gh stimulation, visceral fat reduction. Epithalon is used for telomere extension, anti-aging. These compounds serve different purposes and your choice depends on your primary health goal. Frequently Asked Questions

Which has stronger evidence?

Tesamorelin has fda-approved for hiv lipodystrophy. Epithalon has preclinical + limited clinical (khavinson research). The strength of evidence depends on the specific application you are considering.

Which should I start with?

Start with whichever addresses your most pressing health concern. If you need gh stimulation, start with Tesamorelin. If you need telomere extension, start with Epithalon. A physician can help you prioritize.
