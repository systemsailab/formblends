Sermorelin vs Ipamorelin: Which Is Better?

Quick Answer: Sermorelin vs Ipamorelin compares a two growth hormone secretagogues that work through different receptors. Sermorelin activates the GHRH receptor (amplifies the GH signal). Ipamorelin activates the ghrelin receptor GHS-R (triggers the GH pulse). They use complementary pathways, which is why GH peptide protocols often combine a GHRH analog with a ghrelin mimetic for synergistic effect .

Can You Use Both?

Yes. Sermorelin and Ipamorelin work through independent mechanisms and do not interfere with each other. They can be combined when your health goals span both stimulate natural gh production and selective gh release. Your physician can design a protocol that incorporates both compounds with appropriate timing and dosing.
