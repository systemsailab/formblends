DEA Classification

Ipamorelin is not listed on any schedule of the Controlled Substances Act. Compounding Pharmacy Access

The legal pathway for ipamorelin runs through compounding pharmacies regulated under FDA Sections 503A and 503B. Under these regulations, a licensed physician writes a prescription for ipamorelin based on a valid patient evaluation, and a licensed compounding pharmacy prepares the medication. Section 503A pharmacies compound based on individual prescriptions, while 503B outsourcing facilities can prepare larger batches under FDA oversight. {{CITE:FDA Sections 503A and 503B regulate compounding pharmacy operations}}
