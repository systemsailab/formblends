Cerebrolysin Dosage Guide: Complete Guide

Quick Answer: Cerebrolysin dosage guide depends on the clinical indication. For cognitive enhancement and age-related decline, typical doses range from 5-10 mL given intramuscularly (IM) daily for 10-20 consecutive days. For stroke recovery and TBI, clinical trials have used 30-50 mL intravenously (IV) daily for 10-21 days. Lower IM doses of 2-5 mL are sometimes used for maintenance or milder cognitive concerns. Route of Administration

Intramuscular (IM)

Used for doses up to 10 mL. This is the most common route for outpatient cognitive health protocols. The injection goes into a large muscle (gluteal or deltoid). Doses above 5 mL are typically split between two injection sites to improve comfort.

Intravenous (IV) Infusion

Required for doses above 10 mL. The higher doses used in stroke and TBI protocols (30-50 mL) must be diluted in 100-250 mL of normal saline and infused over 15-60 minutes. IV administration is performed in a clinical setting {{MEDICAL_REVIEW:Cerebrolysin IV administration protocols}}.

Subcutaneous

Not a standard route for Cerebrolysin. The volume of typical doses makes subcutaneous injection impractical. IM is preferred for self-administered protocols.

Treatment Course Structure

Cerebrolysin is given in treatment courses rather than continuously:

  • Active phase: Daily injections for 10-20 consecutive days
  • Rest phase: No injections for 1-3 months
  • Repeat: Another course if benefit was observed

This cycling approach is based on how neurotrophic factors work. They trigger lasting changes in neuronal structure and function that persist beyond the active treatment period. Continuous dosing is not necessary and may not add additional benefit.

Frequently Asked Questions

What dose should beginners start with?

For cognitive optimization in otherwise healthy adults, most physicians start with 5 mL IM daily for 10 days. This lower dose and shorter course allows you to assess tolerance before committing to larger protocols.

Can I inject Cerebrolysin at home?

IM injections of 5 mL or less can be self-administered at home with proper instruction. Higher doses requiring IV infusion must be done in a clinical setting. Your physician will determine which approach is appropriate for your protocol.

How many treatment courses are typical?

For cognitive health maintenance, 2-4 courses per year is common. For neurodegenerative conditions, courses may be repeated more frequently based on clinical response. Each course should be followed by a rest period to allow neurotrophic effects to consolidate.
