TB-500 vs Tesamorelin: Which Is Better?

Quick Answer: TB-500 vs Tesamorelin compares a healing peptide with a GH-stimulating peptide. TB-500 repairs damaged tissues through cell migration and angiogenesis. Tesamorelin is an FDA-approved GHRH analog that stimulates growth hormone release, particularly effective at reducing visceral fat. TB-500 is for tissue healing; Tesamorelin is for body composition and GH optimization .

Can You Use Both?

Yes. TB-500 and Tesamorelin work through independent mechanisms and do not interfere with each other. They can be combined when your health goals span both tissue repair and gh stimulation. Your physician can design a protocol that incorporates both compounds with appropriate timing and dosing.
