Frequently Asked Questions

Is this combination safe?

No direct pharmacological interactions are known between NAD+ and Semaglutide. However, combining any compounds requires physician oversight to monitor for unexpected effects and ensure both are appropriate for your health profile .

Should I start both at the same time?

No. Start with one compound (typically Semaglutide), titrate to your target dose, and stabilize before adding NAD+. This allows clear identification of which compound causes any effects.
