What Is PT-141?

PT-141 is a synthetic peptide derived from the hormone alpha-MSH (alpha-melanocyte-stimulating hormone). Unlike erectile dysfunction medications that work on blood flow, PT-141 works in the brain. It activates melanocortin-4 receptors (MC4R) that are involved in sexual arousal and desire . The FDA approved the brand-name version, Vyleesi, in 2019 for women with hypoactive sexual desire disorder, and physicians also prescribe it off-label for men.

Some people have a strong response on their first use, while others notice a more subtle effect that builds with the second or third use. Do not increase your dose without talking to your provider first.

How do you inject PT-141 for the first time?
