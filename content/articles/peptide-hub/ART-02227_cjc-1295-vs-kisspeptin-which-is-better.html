CJC-1295 vs Kisspeptin: Which Is Better?

Quick Answer: CJC-1295 vs Kisspeptin compares a growth hormone peptide with a reproductive hormone peptide. CJC-1295 stimulates GH from pituitary somatotrophs. Kisspeptin stimulates GnRH from the hypothalamus for reproductive hormone regulation. They target different hormonal axes and can be used concurrently for comprehensive hormone optimization .

Can You Use Both?

Yes. CJC-1295 and Kisspeptin work through independent mechanisms and do not interfere with each other. They can be combined when your health goals span both extended gh stimulation and gnrh stimulation. Your physician can design a protocol that incorporates both compounds with appropriate timing and dosing.

Frequently Asked Questions

Which has stronger evidence?

CJC-1295 has clinical gh elevation data. Kisspeptin has clinical trials in reproductive endocrinology. The strength of evidence depends on the specific application you are considering.

Which should I start with?

Start with whichever addresses your most pressing health concern. If you need extended gh stimulation, start with CJC-1295. If you need gnrh stimulation, start with Kisspeptin. A physician can help you prioritize.
