Ipamorelin vs AOD-9604: Which Is Better?

Quick Answer: Ipamorelin vs AOD-9604 compares a two peptides related to growth hormone but working differently. Ipamorelin triggers natural GH pulses, providing full GH benefits (fat loss, muscle, sleep, skin). AOD-9604 is a synthetic GH fragment providing targeted fat oxidation only. Ipamorelin is broader; AOD-9604 is focused solely on fat metabolism .

Can You Use Both?

Yes. Ipamorelin and AOD-9604 work through independent mechanisms and do not interfere with each other. They can be combined when your health goals span both selective gh release and lipolysis stimulation. Your physician can design a protocol that incorporates both compounds with appropriate timing and dosing.
