When GH Peptides Are Most Impactful for Energy

Patients who benefit most from CJC-1295/Ipamorelin for energy typically share these characteristics:

  • Over 35 with declining sleep quality
  • Lab-confirmed low or low-normal IGF-1
  • Difficulty recovering from exercise
