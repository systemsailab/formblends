Ipamorelin vs GHK-Cu: Which Is Better?

Quick Answer: Ipamorelin vs GHK-Cu compares a selective growth hormone secretagogue with a collagen-stimulating copper peptide. Ipamorelin triggers clean GH pulses for body composition and recovery. GHK-Cu directly stimulates collagen, elastin, and skin rejuvenation. Ipamorelin provides systemic GH benefits; GHK-Cu provides targeted skin and tissue remodeling {{CITE:Ipamorelin and GHK-Cu serve different therapeutic purposes}}.

Can You Use Both?

Yes. Ipamorelin and GHK-Cu work through independent mechanisms and do not interfere with each other. They can be combined when your health goals span both selective gh release and collagen stimulation. Your physician can design a protocol that incorporates both compounds with appropriate timing and dosing.
