Selank for Immune System Support

Selank for immune system support is based on the peptide's anti-inflammatory and metabolic properties, which may create a more favorable environment for immune function. How Metabolic Health Affects Immunity

Your immune system depends on metabolic health:

  • Immune cells require significant energy to mount effective responses {{CITE:Immune cell activation requires substantial increases in cellular energy production}}
  • Chronic inflammation suppresses adaptive immune function
  • Insulin resistance impairs immune cell function and increases infection susceptibility

Selank may support immunity indirectly by optimizing the metabolic foundation that immune cells depend on.

Selank and Inflammation

Selank has demonstrated anti-inflammatory effects in preclinical models, reducing pro-inflammatory cytokines. This matters for immunity because:

  • Chronic inflammation diverts immune resources away from pathogen defense
  • Excessive inflammatory signaling can cause immunosuppression over time
  • Balanced inflammation supports both innate and adaptive immune responses
