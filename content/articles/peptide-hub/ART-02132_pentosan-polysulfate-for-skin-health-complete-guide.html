Pentosan Polysulfate For Skin Health: Complete Guide

Quick Answer: Pentosan Polysulfate for skin health is not a recognized application. PPS is designed for joint cartilage protection and bladder GAG layer restoration. While GAG molecules exist in skin tissue as part of the extracellular matrix, PPS has not been studied for skin-specific benefits. Why PPS Is Not for Skin

  • No studies on PPS for skin conditions or skin aging
  • PPS targets cartilage-specific GAG structures, not dermal GAGs
  • Injectable PPS does not preferentially distribute to skin tissue

Frequently Asked Questions

Should I use PPS for skin concerns?

No. Choose skin-specific peptides like GHK-Cu for aging skin, KPV for inflammatory skin conditions, or TB-4 for wound healing.

Can PPS cause skin side effects?

Injection site reactions (redness, mild irritation) are possible but resolve quickly. PPS does not cause systemic skin changes.
