Cerebrolysin Storage Instructions: Complete Guide

Quick Answer: Cerebrolysin storage instructions are simple: store at room temperature (59-77F / 15-25C), protect from light, and do not freeze. Cerebrolysin is more stable than many peptide preparations and does not require refrigeration when unopened. Once an ampoule is opened, use the contents immediately. Multi-dose vials should be refrigerated after opening and used within 28 days. Always check the expiration date before use .

Ampoule vs Vial Storage

Ampoules

Single-use ampoules are the most common Cerebrolysin format. Once the ampoule is snapped open, the contents should be used immediately. Do not store an opened ampoule for later use, as sterility cannot be maintained after opening.

Multi-Dose Vials

Larger vials (e.g., 30 mL) with rubber stoppers can be used across multiple doses. After the first needle puncture, refrigerate the vial and use within 28 days. Clean the stopper with alcohol before each use.

Signs of Degraded Cerebrolysin

  • Color change: Normal Cerebrolysin is clear amber-yellow. Significant darkening or discoloration indicates degradation
  • Cloudiness or particles: Any turbidity or visible particles warrants disposal
  • Unusual odor: Cerebrolysin has a mild characteristic odor. Strong or unusual smells indicate contamination
  • Past expiration: Do not use expired Cerebrolysin

Travel and Transport

  • Unopened ampoules can travel at room temperature in a padded case
  • Keep away from extreme heat (car dashboards, direct sunlight)
  • For air travel, keep Cerebrolysin in carry-on with your prescription documentation
  • Pack ampoules in a protective case to prevent breakage

Frequently Asked Questions

Does Cerebrolysin need to be refrigerated?

Unopened ampoules do not require refrigeration. Room temperature storage is appropriate. Only opened multi-dose vials need refrigeration. Some patients prefer to store all Cerebrolysin in the refrigerator for consistency, which is acceptable but not required.

What happens if Cerebrolysin gets warm?

Brief temperature excursions (a few hours at warm room temperature) are unlikely to damage Cerebrolysin. Prolonged heat exposure above 77F (25C) or temperatures above 104F (40C) could accelerate degradation. If in doubt, contact your pharmacy.

Can I take Cerebrolysin on a multi-day trip?

Yes. Pack your ampoules or vial in a padded travel case. Ampoules are ideal for travel since each is a single use with no refrigeration needed. Bring alcohol swabs, syringes, and a sharps container.
