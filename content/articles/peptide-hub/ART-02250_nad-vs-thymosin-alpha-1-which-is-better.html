NAD+ vs Thymosin Alpha-1: Which Is Better?

Quick Answer: NAD+ vs Thymosin Alpha-1 compares a coenzyme (not a peptide) with a immune-modulating peptide. NAD+ is used for cellular energy, dna repair, sirtuin activation. Thymosin Alpha-1 is used for immune enhancement. These compounds serve different purposes and your choice depends on your primary health goal.
