Sermorelin vs GHK-Cu: Which Is Better?

Quick Answer: Sermorelin vs GHK-Cu compares a growth hormone peptide with a collagen-stimulating copper peptide. Sermorelin boosts GH production for systemic benefits including body composition, sleep, and recovery. GHK-Cu directly stimulates collagen, elastin, and glycosaminoglycan production for skin rejuvenation and wound remodeling. Sermorelin provides broad systemic GH benefits; GHK-Cu provides targeted skin and tissue remodeling {{CITE:Sermorelin and GHK-Cu serve different therapeutic purposes}}.
