Sermorelin vs AOD-9604: Which Is Better?

Quick Answer: Sermorelin vs AOD-9604 compares a two peptides that both affect body composition but through different mechanisms. Sermorelin stimulates full GH release, providing broad benefits including fat loss, muscle support, sleep, and recovery. AOD-9604 is a GH fragment that targets fat oxidation specifically without other GH effects.
