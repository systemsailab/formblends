Cerebrolysin For Skin Health: Complete Guide

Quick Answer: Cerebrolysin for skin health is not an appropriate application. Cerebrolysin is a neurotrophic peptide preparation that targets neurons and brain tissue exclusively. It contains no growth factors relevant to skin cell turnover, collagen production, or dermal repair. For skin health, growth hormone peptides like CJC-1295/Ipamorelin for skin health that boost collagen synthesis, or tissue repair peptides like BPC-157 for skin health and TB-4 for skin health, offer evidence-based skin benefits .

Why Cerebrolysin Does Not Help Skin

  • Cerebrolysin's BDNF-like and NGF-like factors act on neurons, not keratinocytes or fibroblasts (skin cells)
  • Skin health requires collagen stimulation, elastin production, and epidermal turnover, none of which are Cerebrolysin mechanisms
  • No research has examined Cerebrolysin for any dermatological application
