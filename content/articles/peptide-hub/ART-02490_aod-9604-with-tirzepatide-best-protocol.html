Frequently Asked Questions

Is this combination safe?

No direct pharmacological interactions are known between AOD-9604 and Tirzepatide. However, combining any compounds requires physician oversight to monitor for unexpected effects and ensure both are appropriate for your health profile {{MEDICAL_REVIEW:AOD-9604 + Tirzepatide safety}}.

Should I start both at the same time?

No. Start with one compound (typically Tirzepatide), titrate to your target dose, and stabilize before adding AOD-9604. This allows clear identification of which compound causes any effects.
