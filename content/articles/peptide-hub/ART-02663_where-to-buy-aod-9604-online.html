Red Flags to Avoid

The online peptide market includes many questionable sellers. Watch for these warning signs:

  • No COA available: Any seller who cannot provide a certificate of analysis should be avoided entirely.
  • Unusually low prices: If AOD-9604 is priced dramatically below market rates, product quality is likely compromised. {{VERIFY_PRICE:AOD-9604}}
  • Health claims on the website: Legitimate research suppliers do not make therapeutic claims about their products. Websites advertising weight loss or fat-burning benefits are marketing an unapproved drug use.

  • Pre-mixed solutions: Research peptides should arrive as lyophilized powder, not pre-reconstituted solutions, which have shorter stability and higher contamination risk.
