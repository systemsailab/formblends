Ipamorelin vs PT-141: Which Is Better?

Quick Answer: Ipamorelin vs PT-141 compares a selective growth hormone secretagogue with an FDA-approved sexual function peptide. Ipamorelin stimulates GH for body composition, sleep, and recovery. PT-141 activates melanocortin receptors for sexual desire and arousal. They address different aspects of vitality and wellness .
