Before and After by Condition

Tendon and Ligament Injuries

Before: Pain with movement, limited range of motion, swelling around the tendon or joint. MRI or ultrasound showing partial tear or chronic degeneration.

After (8-12 weeks): Reduced or eliminated pain, restored range of motion, decreased swelling. Follow-up imaging often shows improved tendon structure and reduced inflammation. Many patients return to activities they had stopped due to pain .

Wound Healing

Before: Open or slow-healing wound, prolonged post-surgical recovery, excessive scarring.

After (2-6 weeks): Faster wound closure, reduced scarring, improved skin quality at the wound site. Surgical patients often notice recovery proceeding ahead of the typical timeline their surgeon outlined.

Hair Growth

Before: Thinning hair, visible scalp, reduced hair density in pattern-loss areas.

After (3-6 months): New vellus hairs appearing in thinning areas, existing hairs appearing thicker and healthier. Full cosmetic improvement may take 4 to 6 months of consistent use. Results are most noticeable in patients with recent-onset thinning rather than long-standing baldness.

Athletic Recovery

Before: Prolonged soreness after training, slow recovery between sessions, nagging minor injuries that linger.

After (4-8 weeks): Faster recovery between training sessions, resolution of chronic minor injuries, improved ability to maintain training intensity. Athletes often report being able to train more frequently with less downtime.

Factors That Influence Results

  • Age: Younger patients generally heal faster, but TB-4 can still significantly accelerate healing in older adults
  • Injury severity: Mild to moderate injuries respond more dramatically than severe damage
  • Overall health: Good nutrition, adequate sleep, and controlled inflammation optimize TB-4 results
  • Compliance: Consistent dosing throughout the treatment course is important. Missed doses slow the cumulative healing effect
  • Complementary therapy: Physical therapy, proper nutrition, and complementary peptides (like BPC-157) amplify TB-4 results

Setting Realistic Expectations

TB-4 is a powerful healing peptide, but it is not magic:

  • It accelerates your body's natural healing capacity rather than creating new healing mechanisms
  • Complete tendon tears or severe structural damage may still require surgery
  • Hair growth results are best for early-stage thinning, not long-established baldness
  • Results vary significantly between individuals based on biology, injury type, and overall health
  • Most benefits require a full treatment course (8-12 weeks) to fully manifest

Frequently Asked Questions

Do results last after stopping TB-4?

Yes. Tissue that has been repaired remains repaired. Healed tendons, closed wounds, and regenerated tissue are permanent structural changes. However, TB-4 does not prevent future injuries, so maintaining healthy movement patterns and conditioning is important.

Can I measure progress objectively?

Yes. Depending on your condition, tracking methods include: MRI/ultrasound imaging for tendon and ligament injuries, wound measurements and photographs for healing, pain scores (1-10 scale), range of motion measurements, and trichoscopy or hair density analysis for hair growth.

What if I do not see results after 4 weeks?

Some conditions take longer to respond. Discuss with your physician whether a dose adjustment, extended treatment course, or combination with other peptides may be appropriate. Not all injuries respond equally to TB-4.
