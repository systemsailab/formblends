Why You Feel Drained

Low energy and chronic fatigue are among the most common health complaints worldwide. While the causes are varied, several biological factors consistently drain energy:

  • Chronic inflammation: The body diverts significant metabolic resources to sustaining an inflammatory response. This "invisible tax" on your system leaves less energy available for daily activities, thinking, and performance
  • Impaired tissue repair: Unresolved injuries, lingering muscle damage, and poorly healed connective tissue force the body into a constant state of attempted repair, which is energetically expensive
  • Poor circulation: When blood flow is compromised, oxygen and nutrient delivery to cells is reduced, directly impacting cellular energy production
  • Sleep disruption: Pain and inflammation that interrupt sleep prevent the body from fully recharging
  • Oxidative stress: An excess of reactive oxygen species damages mitochondria, the energy-producing organelles within your cells

Frequently Asked Questions

Can TB-500 help with chronic fatigue syndrome?

Chronic fatigue syndrome (CFS/ME) is a complex condition with multiple contributing factors, many of which involve inflammation and immune dysfunction. While there is no clinical trial data for TB-500 in CFS, its anti-inflammatory and tissue-repair properties address several pathways implicated in the condition. Discuss this with your physician to determine if TB-500 is appropriate for your situation .

How is TB-500 energy improvement different from exercise energy improvement?

Exercise improves energy through cardiovascular fitness, mitochondrial biogenesis, and hormonal optimization. TB-500 improves energy by reducing inflammation, repairing tissue, and enhancing blood flow. These mechanisms are complementary, and using TB-500 alongside a regular exercise program often produces the best results.

Do energy benefits last after I stop TB-500?

Many users report that energy improvements persist during and after the rest period, especially if the fatigue was driven by inflammation or tissue damage that TB-500 helped resolve. Ongoing benefits depend on whether the underlying causes have been addressed or are chronic and recurring.
