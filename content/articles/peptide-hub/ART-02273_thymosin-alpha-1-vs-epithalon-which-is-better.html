Thymosin Alpha-1 vs Epithalon: Which Is Better?

Quick Answer: Thymosin Alpha-1 vs Epithalon compares a immune-modulating peptide with a telomerase-activating peptide. Thymosin Alpha-1 is used for immune enhancement. Epithalon is used for telomere extension, anti-aging. These compounds serve different purposes and your choice depends on your primary health goal. They can often be used together since they work through independent mechanisms .
