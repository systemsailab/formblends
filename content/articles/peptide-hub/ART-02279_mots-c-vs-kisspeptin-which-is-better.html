MOTS-c vs Kisspeptin: Which Is Better?

Quick Answer: MOTS-c vs Kisspeptin compares a mitochondrial-derived peptide with a reproductive hormone peptide. MOTS-c is used for metabolic optimization, exercise enhancement. Kisspeptin is used for gnrh stimulation, reproductive hormone regulation. These compounds serve different purposes and your choice depends on your primary health goal. Can You Use Both?

Yes. MOTS-c and Kisspeptin work through independent mechanisms and do not interfere with each other. They can be combined when your health goals span both metabolic optimization and gnrh stimulation. Your physician can design a protocol that incorporates both compounds with appropriate timing and dosing.
