AOD-9604 with Glp-1: Stacking Guide

Frequently Asked Questions

Is it safe to combine AOD-9604 with semaglutide?

There are no known direct pharmacological interactions between AOD-9604 and GLP-1 agonists. However, any combination should be supervised by a physician who can monitor your response and adjust dosing as needed .

Should I start both at the same time?

No. We recommend starting one compound first (typically the GLP-1 medication), stabilizing on that dose, and then adding the second compound. This allows you to identify which compound causes any side effects and adjust accordingly.
