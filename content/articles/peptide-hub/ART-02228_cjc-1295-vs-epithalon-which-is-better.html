CJC-1295 vs Epithalon: Which Is Better?

Quick Answer: CJC-1295 vs Epithalon compares a growth hormone peptide with a telomerase-activating anti-aging peptide. CJC-1295 optimizes GH for body composition and recovery. Epithalon activates telomerase for telomere protection and regulates melatonin. Both support longevity but at different biological levels: hormonal vs chromosomal .

Can You Use Both?

Yes. CJC-1295 and Epithalon work through independent mechanisms and do not interfere with each other. They can be combined when your health goals span both extended gh stimulation and telomere extension. Your physician can design a protocol that incorporates both compounds with appropriate timing and dosing.

Frequently Asked Questions

CJC-1295 has clinical gh elevation data. Epithalon has preclinical + limited clinical (khavinson research). The strength of evidence depends on the specific application you are considering.

Which should I start with?

Start with whichever addresses your most pressing health concern. If you need extended gh stimulation, start with CJC-1295. If you need telomere extension, start with Epithalon. A physician can help you prioritize.
