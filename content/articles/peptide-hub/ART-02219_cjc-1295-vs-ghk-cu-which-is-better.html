CJC-1295 vs GHK-Cu: Which Is Better?

Quick Answer: CJC-1295 vs GHK-Cu compares a growth hormone peptide with a collagen-stimulating copper peptide. CJC-1295 boosts systemic GH/IGF-1 for broad anti-aging, body composition, and recovery benefits. GHK-Cu directly stimulates collagen and elastin production for skin rejuvenation and wound remodeling. CJC-1295 provides systemic hormonal benefits that include some skin improvement; GHK-Cu provides more potent, targeted skin-specific effects .

Can You Use Both?

Yes. CJC-1295 and GHK-Cu work through independent mechanisms and do not interfere with each other. They can be combined when your health goals span both extended gh stimulation and collagen stimulation. Your physician can design a protocol that incorporates both compounds with appropriate timing and dosing.

Frequently Asked Questions

Which has stronger evidence?

CJC-1295 has clinical gh elevation data. GHK-Cu has preclinical + clinical (topical). The strength of evidence depends on the specific application you are considering.

Which should I start with?

Start with whichever addresses your most pressing health concern. If you need extended gh stimulation, start with CJC-1295. If you need collagen stimulation, start with GHK-Cu. A physician can help you prioritize.
