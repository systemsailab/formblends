TB-500 vs AOD-9604: Which Is Better?

Quick Answer: TB-500 vs AOD-9604 compares a tissue healing peptide with a fat-loss peptide. TB-500 (Thymosin Beta-4) promotes wound healing, cell migration, and tissue repair throughout the body. AOD-9604 is a growth hormone fragment that stimulates fat breakdown without the growth-promoting effects of full GH. These peptides serve completely different purposes: TB-500 for healing and recovery, AOD-9604 for body composition improvement. Frequently Asked Questions

Can TB-500 help with body composition?

TB-500 does not directly affect fat metabolism. However, by supporting tissue repair and reducing inflammation, it can help you return to full training capacity faster, which indirectly supports body composition goals through exercise.

Is AOD-9604 as effective as semaglutide for weight loss?

No. Semaglutide produces significantly greater weight loss (15-20% body weight in trials). AOD-9604 provides more modest, localized fat reduction. For meaningful weight loss, GLP-1 agonists are more effective.
