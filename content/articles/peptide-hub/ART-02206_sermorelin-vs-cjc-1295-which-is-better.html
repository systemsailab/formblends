Sermorelin vs CJC-1295: Which Is Better?

Quick Answer: Sermorelin vs CJC-1295 compares a two growth hormone secretagogues that work through the same receptor but differ in pharmacokinetics. Both are GHRH analogs that stimulate pituitary GH release. Sermorelin (GRF 1-29) has a shorter half-life of 10-20 minutes. CJC-1295, especially with DAC, has a half-life of days, providing more sustained GH elevation. CJC-1295 has largely replaced Sermorelin in clinical practice due to more convenient dosing .
