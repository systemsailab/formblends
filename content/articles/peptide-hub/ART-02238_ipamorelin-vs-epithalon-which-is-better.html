Ipamorelin vs Epithalon: Which Is Better?

Quick Answer: Ipamorelin vs Epithalon compares a selective growth hormone secretagogue with a telomerase-activating anti-aging peptide. Ipamorelin optimizes GH for body composition and vitality. Epithalon protects telomeres and regulates melatonin production. Both support longevity through different biological mechanisms .
