Red Flags to Watch For

The peptide space has its share of bad actors. Here are warning signs that a source is not legitimate:

  • No provider interaction: If the entire transaction happens without a single conversation with a healthcare professional, the product and process are suspect.
  • Unusually low prices: Pharmaceutical-grade compounded sermorelin has real costs. Prices far below market rate usually indicate compromised quality.
