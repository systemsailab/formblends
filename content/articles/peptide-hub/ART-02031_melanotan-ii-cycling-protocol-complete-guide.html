Melanotan II Cycling Protocol: Complete Guide

Quick Answer: The standard Melanotan II cycling protocol involves a loading phase of daily injections for 2 to 4 weeks until desired pigmentation is reached, followed by a maintenance phase of 1 to 2 injections per week. Unlike some peptides that require strict on/off cycling, Melanotan II uses a "load then maintain" approach because its primary effect (melanin production) needs sustained signaling to persist .

Loading Phase

Fair-skinned individuals (Fitzpatrick I-II) typically need the full 3 to 4 weeks. Medium-toned skin (Type III) may reach desired pigmentation in 1 to 2 weeks. Stop the loading phase when you reach your target. Continuing beyond your desired level wastes product and increases unnecessary side effect exposure .

Maintenance Phase

Once your desired tan is established, switch to maintenance dosing:

  • Fair skin (Types I-II): 250-500 mcg twice per week
  • Medium skin (Type III): 250 mcg once or twice per week
  • Olive skin (Type IV): 100-250 mcg once per week

The maintenance phase can continue indefinitely as long as you remain under physician supervision and maintain regular dermatological monitoring. Many users find they can gradually reduce their maintenance dose over time while retaining pigmentation .

Seasonal Cycling

Some users adopt a seasonal approach:

  • Spring: Begin loading phase (2-4 weeks) to establish base tan before summer
  • Summer: Low maintenance dose, supplemented by natural sun exposure
  • Fall: Continue maintenance or gradually reduce
  • Winter: Either maintain at standard dose or take a break (off-cycle) and restart in spring

Taking a winter off-cycle of 2 to 3 months gives your body a break from melanocortin stimulation and reduces cumulative side effect exposure. Pigmentation fades over 4 to 8 weeks after stopping.

Off-Cycle Considerations

During off-cycle periods:

  • Pigmentation gradually fades as melanin-containing cells shed through normal skin turnover
  • Side effects (nausea, flushing) resolve within days of stopping
  • Libido and appetite return to baseline within 1 to 2 weeks
  • Continue dermatological monitoring, as mole changes can persist
  • When restarting after a break, you may need a brief loading phase (1-2 weeks) rather than the full initial load

Signs You Need to Adjust

  • Getting too dark: Reduce dose or increase time between maintenance injections
  • Persistent nausea: Lower your dose and ensure bedtime injection
  • New mole or mole changes: Stop use and see a dermatologist before continuing
  • Tolerance to sexual effects: Consider a 2 to 4 week break to resensitize receptors
  • Uneven pigmentation: Brief, controlled UV exposure can help even color; do not increase dose

Frequently Asked Questions

How long should I load Melanotan II?

Until you reach your desired pigmentation, typically 2 to 4 weeks. Do not extend loading beyond 4 weeks without physician reassessment.

Can I use Melanotan II continuously without breaks?

Continuous maintenance dosing is possible with physician oversight, but seasonal cycling (with winter breaks) reduces cumulative exposure and gives your body periodic rest from melanocortin stimulation.

How quickly does my tan fade after stopping?

Pigmentation fades over 4 to 8 weeks after stopping, as melanin-containing skin cells naturally shed. Individual rates depend on skin turnover speed, which varies by age and skin type.

Do I need to reload after every break?

After a short break (2-4 weeks), you may resume at maintenance dose. After longer breaks (2+ months), a brief re-loading phase of 1 to 2 weeks at daily dosing typically restores pigmentation faster than starting from scratch.
