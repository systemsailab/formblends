GHK-Cu vs Kisspeptin: Which Is Better?

Quick Answer: GHK-Cu vs Kisspeptin compares a tissue remodeling peptide with a reproductive hormone peptide. GHK-Cu is used for collagen stimulation, skin rejuvenation. Kisspeptin is used for gnrh stimulation, reproductive hormone regulation. These compounds serve different purposes and your choice depends on your primary health goal. Can You Use Both?

Yes. GHK-Cu and Kisspeptin work through independent mechanisms and do not interfere with each other. They can be combined when your health goals span both collagen stimulation and gnrh stimulation. Your physician can design a protocol that incorporates both compounds with appropriate timing and dosing.

GHK-Cu has preclinical + clinical (topical). Kisspeptin has clinical trials in reproductive endocrinology. The strength of evidence depends on the specific application you are considering.

Which should I start with?

Start with whichever addresses your most pressing health concern. If you need collagen stimulation, start with GHK-Cu. If you need gnrh stimulation, start with Kisspeptin. A physician can help you prioritize.
