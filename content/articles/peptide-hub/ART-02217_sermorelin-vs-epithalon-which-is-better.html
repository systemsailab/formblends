Sermorelin vs Epithalon: Which Is Better?

Quick Answer: Sermorelin vs Epithalon compares a growth hormone peptide with a telomerase-activating anti-aging peptide. Sermorelin optimizes GH for body composition and recovery. Epithalon activates telomerase to maintain telomere length and regulates melatonin production. Both are anti-aging compounds but work at different biological levels: hormonal optimization vs chromosomal protection {{CITE:Sermorelin and Epithalon serve different therapeutic purposes}}.
