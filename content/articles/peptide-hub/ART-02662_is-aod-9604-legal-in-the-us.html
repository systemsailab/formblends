What Is AOD-9604?

AOD-9604 is a modified fragment of human growth hormone (HGH), consisting of amino acids 177 through 191 with an added tyrosine at the N-terminal. It was developed by Monash University in Australia as a potential anti-obesity treatment. The peptide is designed to stimulate lipolysis (fat breakdown) and inhibit lipogenesis (fat formation) without the growth-promoting or diabetogenic effects of full-length growth hormone. {{CITE:AOD-9604 structure and development}} {{MEDICAL_REVIEW:AOD-9604 mechanism of action}}
