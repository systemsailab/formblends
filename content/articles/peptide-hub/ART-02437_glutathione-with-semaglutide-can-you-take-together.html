Frequently Asked Questions

Is this combination safe?

No direct pharmacological interactions are known between Glutathione and Semaglutide. However, combining any compounds requires physician oversight to monitor for unexpected effects and ensure both are appropriate for your health profile .

Should I start both at the same time?

No. Start with one compound (typically Semaglutide), titrate to your target dose, and stabilize before adding Glutathione. This allows clear identification of which compound causes any effects.
