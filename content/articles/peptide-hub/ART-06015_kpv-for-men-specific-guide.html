How Men Respond Differently

Men have distinct hormonal profiles driven by testosterone, DHEA, and growth hormone. These hormones influence metabolism, fat distribution, muscle mass, and how the body responds to therapeutic interventions .

Research indicates that men may experience different patterns in terms of both benefits and side effects. Understanding these differences helps physicians tailor protocols for better outcomes.
