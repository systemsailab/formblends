Thymosin Alpha-1 vs MOTS-c: Which Is Better?

Quick Answer: Thymosin Alpha-1 vs MOTS-c compares a immune-modulating peptide with a mitochondrial-derived peptide. Thymosin Alpha-1 is used for immune enhancement. MOTS-c is used for metabolic optimization, exercise enhancement. These compounds serve different purposes and your choice depends on your primary health goal. They can often be used together since they work through independent mechanisms .
