CJC-1295 vs Thymosin Alpha-1: Which Is Better?

Quick Answer: CJC-1295 vs Thymosin Alpha-1 compares a growth hormone peptide with an immune-modulating peptide. CJC-1295 stimulates pituitary GH release for body composition, sleep, and recovery. Thymosin Alpha-1 enhances immune function through T-cell maturation and antiviral defense. They target completely different systems: endocrine (GH) vs immune .
