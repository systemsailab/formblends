NAD+ vs PT-141: Which Is Better?

Quick Answer: NAD+ vs PT-141 compares a coenzyme (not a peptide) with a sexual function peptide. NAD+ is used for cellular energy, dna repair, sirtuin activation. PT-141 is used for sexual desire and arousal enhancement. These compounds serve different purposes and your choice depends on your primary health goal.
