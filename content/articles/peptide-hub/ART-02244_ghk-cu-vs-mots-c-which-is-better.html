GHK-Cu vs MOTS-c: Which Is Better?

Quick Answer: GHK-Cu vs MOTS-c compares a tissue remodeling peptide with a mitochondrial-derived peptide. GHK-Cu is used for collagen stimulation, skin rejuvenation. MOTS-c is used for metabolic optimization, exercise enhancement. These compounds serve different purposes and your choice depends on your primary health goal. They can often be used together since they work through independent mechanisms .

Can You Use Both?

Yes. GHK-Cu and MOTS-c work through independent mechanisms and do not interfere with each other. They can be combined when your health goals span both collagen stimulation and metabolic optimization. Your physician can design a protocol that incorporates both compounds with appropriate timing and dosing.
