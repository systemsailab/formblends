Tesamorelin vs Kisspeptin: Which Is Better?

Quick Answer: Tesamorelin vs Kisspeptin compares a growth hormone secretagogue with a reproductive hormone peptide. Tesamorelin is used for gh stimulation, visceral fat reduction. Kisspeptin is used for gnrh stimulation, reproductive hormone regulation. These compounds serve different purposes and your choice depends on your primary health goal. Can You Use Both?

Yes. Tesamorelin and Kisspeptin work through independent mechanisms and do not interfere with each other. They can be combined when your health goals span both gh stimulation and gnrh stimulation. Your physician can design a protocol that incorporates both compounds with appropriate timing and dosing.
