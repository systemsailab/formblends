GHK-Cu vs PT-141: Which Is Better?

Quick Answer: GHK-Cu vs PT-141 compares a collagen-stimulating copper peptide with an FDA-approved sexual function peptide. GHK-Cu targets skin rejuvenation and tissue remodeling. PT-141 targets sexual desire through melanocortin receptor activation. They serve entirely unrelated functions and would only be used together if both skin and sexual health goals exist simultaneously {{CITE:GHK-Cu and PT-141 serve different therapeutic purposes}}.

Can You Use Both?

Yes. GHK-Cu and PT-141 work through independent mechanisms and do not interfere with each other. They can be combined when your health goals span both collagen stimulation and sexual desire and arousal enhancement. Your physician can design a protocol that incorporates both compounds with appropriate timing and dosing.

Frequently Asked Questions

GHK-Cu has preclinical + clinical (topical). PT-141 has fda-approved (vyleesi). The strength of evidence depends on the specific application you are considering.

Which should I start with?

Start with whichever addresses your most pressing health concern. If you need collagen stimulation, start with GHK-Cu. If you need sexual desire and arousal enhancement, start with PT-141. A physician can help you prioritize.
