Joint Health and Cellular Energy

Cartilage cells (chondrocytes) depend on mitochondria for the energy needed to maintain the extracellular matrix. In osteoarthritis, mitochondrial dysfunction in chondrocytes is well-documented {{CITE:Mitochondrial dysfunction in chondrocytes is a documented feature of osteoarthritis}}.
