TB-500 vs Epithalon: Which Is Better?

Quick Answer: TB-500 vs Epithalon compares a tissue repair peptide with a telomerase-activating anti-aging peptide. TB-500 heals specific tissue damage through cell migration and wound healing mechanisms. Epithalon (Epitalon) activates telomerase to extend telomeres and regulates pineal gland function for melatonin production. TB-500 repairs current damage; Epithalon addresses cellular aging at the DNA level {{CITE:TB-500 and Epithalon serve different therapeutic purposes}}.
