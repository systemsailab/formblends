Who Qualifies for GLP-1 Therapy?

GLP-1 medications are prescribed based on clinical criteria established by FDA guidelines. You may be eligible if:

  • Your BMI is 30 or higher
  • Your BMI is 27 or higher and you have at least one weight-related comorbidity, such as type 2 diabetes, hypertension, or obstructive sleep apnea
  • Past efforts to lose weight through diet and exercise have not delivered lasting results
  • You are not currently pregnant, breastfeeding, or planning to conceive
  • You have no personal or family history of medullary thyroid carcinoma or MEN2 syndrome

Your provider will assess your eligibility thoroughly during your consultation and only recommend GLP-1 therapy if it is clinically appropriate.
