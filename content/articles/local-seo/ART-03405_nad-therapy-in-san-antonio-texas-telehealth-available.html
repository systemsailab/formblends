What Is NAD+ and Why Does It Matter?

Nicotinamide adenine dinucleotide (NAD+) is a coenzyme found in every living cell. It plays a central role in energy metabolism, DNA repair, and cellular signaling. As we age, NAD+ levels naturally decline, which researchers believe contributes to many of the symptoms associated with getting older: fatigue, cognitive slowdown, and reduced physical resilience. {{CITE:NAD+ age-related decline research}}

NAD+ therapy aims to restore these declining levels through supplementation, supporting the biological processes that keep your cells functioning well.
