Why GLP-1 Medications Are Changing Weight Loss

GLP-1 receptor agonists have transformed how physicians approach weight management. Originally developed for type 2 diabetes, medications like semaglutide and tirzepatide have shown remarkable weight loss results in clinical trials. Patients typically see 15% to 20% body weight reduction when combined with lifestyle changes.

These medications work by mimicking a natural hormone that regulates appetite and blood sugar. They slow gastric emptying, reduce hunger signals, and help you feel satisfied with smaller portions. Who Is a Good Candidate for Telehealth Weight Loss?

Our physicians evaluate every patient individually, but telehealth weight loss with GLP-1 medications is generally appropriate for adults who:

  • Have a BMI of 30 or higher, or a BMI of 27 or higher with at least one weight-related health condition
  • Have struggled to achieve lasting results through diet and exercise alone
  • Are not pregnant or planning to become pregnant
  • Do not have a personal or family history of medullary thyroid carcinoma or MEN 2 syndrome
