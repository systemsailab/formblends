Who Is a Good Candidate for Semaglutide?

Semaglutide is prescribed for adults who meet specific clinical criteria. You may qualify if:

  • Your BMI is 30 or above, which is classified as obesity
  • Your BMI is 27 or above and you have at least one weight-related comorbidity such as hypertension, dyslipidemia, or type 2 diabetes
  • You have tried to lose weight through lifestyle modifications without sustained success
  • You are not pregnant, breastfeeding, or planning pregnancy in the near term

Semaglutide is not recommended for individuals with a history of pancreatitis, medullary thyroid carcinoma, or MEN2 syndrome . Your Form Blends provider will screen for all contraindications during your consultation.
