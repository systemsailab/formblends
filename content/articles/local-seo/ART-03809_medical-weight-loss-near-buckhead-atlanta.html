Eligibility for Medical Weight Loss

Medical weight loss with GLP-1 medications is appropriate for adults who meet established clinical criteria:

  • BMI of 30 or higher
  • BMI of 27 or higher with at least one weight-related health condition, such as type 2 diabetes, hypertension, or elevated cholesterol
  • Documented difficulty achieving lasting weight loss through diet and exercise
  • No current pregnancy or plans to become pregnant during treatment
  • No personal or family history of medullary thyroid carcinoma or MEN2 syndrome

Your Form Blends provider will review your complete health profile during your consultation and determine whether GLP-1 therapy is the right path for you.
