Who Is Eligible for Tirzepatide?

Tirzepatide is prescribed for adults who meet established medical criteria for weight management. You may qualify if:

  • You have a BMI of 30 or greater {{CITE:claim}}
  • You have a BMI of 27 or greater along with at least one weight-related health condition such as type 2 diabetes, hypertension, or obstructive sleep apnea
  • You have not achieved lasting results through diet and exercise alone
  • You are not pregnant or planning to become pregnant during treatment

Tirzepatide is contraindicated for patients with a personal or family history of medullary thyroid carcinoma or MEN2 syndrome {{CITE:claim}}. Your provider will screen for all relevant contraindications before prescribing.
