Frequently Asked Questions

How do I know if my hormones are off?

Common signs include persistent tiredness, difficulty losing weight, decreased sex drive, mood changes, poor sleep, and brain fog. The only way to confirm an imbalance is through bloodwork, which your Form Blends physician will order as part of the evaluation.

Is hormone optimization the same as hormone replacement therapy?

They are related but not identical. Hormone replacement typically refers to restoring hormones to baseline levels. Hormone optimization aims for levels that support peak function and quality of life, which may differ from what a standard lab report calls "normal" .

Are there risks to hormone therapy?

All medical treatments carry some risk. Physician-supervised hormone optimization with regular monitoring significantly reduces the chance of adverse effects. Your doctor discusses potential risks specific to your protocol during the consultation .

Can men and women both benefit from hormone optimization?

Absolutely. Hormonal imbalances affect both sexes. Women may benefit from optimizing testosterone, thyroid, and progesterone levels. Men commonly benefit from testosterone and thyroid optimization. Your physician tailors the approach to your biology.
