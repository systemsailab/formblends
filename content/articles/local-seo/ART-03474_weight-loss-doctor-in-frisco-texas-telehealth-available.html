What Makes a Good Weight Loss Doctor

Not every doctor who offers weight loss services has the same training or approach. When you are searching for a weight loss doctor in the Frisco area, here is what matters:

  • Medical licensure: Your provider should be a licensed MD or DO, not a nurse practitioner working without physician oversight or an unlicensed "wellness coach."
  • Evidence-based approach: The best weight loss doctors rely on peer-reviewed research and FDA-recognized medications, not supplements or fad protocols.
  • Individualized care: Your body, health history, and goals are unique. A good doctor treats you accordingly.
  • Ongoing monitoring: Weight loss is not a one-visit event. Look for a provider who schedules regular follow-ups and adjusts your treatment over time.
