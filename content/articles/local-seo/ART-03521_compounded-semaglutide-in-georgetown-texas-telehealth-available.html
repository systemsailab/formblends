How Compounded Semaglutide Works

Semaglutide works by mimicking GLP-1, a hormone that regulates appetite and blood sugar. When you take semaglutide, it:

  • Reduces hunger signals sent to the brain
  • Slows gastric emptying, helping you feel full longer
  • Improves insulin sensitivity and blood sugar regulation
  • May reduce food cravings, particularly for high-calorie foods
