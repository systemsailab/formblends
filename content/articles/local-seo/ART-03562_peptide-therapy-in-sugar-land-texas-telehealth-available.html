What Is Peptide Therapy?

Peptides are naturally occurring short-chain amino acid sequences that function as signaling molecules throughout the body. Therapeutic peptides are designed to support specific biological processes including tissue repair, immune regulation, metabolic optimization, and hormone signaling. {{CITE:peptide therapy science}} {{MEDICAL_REVIEW:peptide biological mechanisms}}

Unlike broad-spectrum supplements, peptide therapy targets precise pathways. Each compound is selected based on your health profile and goals, making it a highly individualized approach to wellness.
