The Advantages of Telehealth for Weight Loss

Choosing telehealth for your weight loss program offers several practical advantages:

  • No commute. Save time and gas by attending appointments from home, your office, or anywhere with a private internet connection.
  • Consistent access. Telehealth makes it easier to attend every follow-up, which improves treatment outcomes. Patients who maintain regular contact with their provider tend to lose more weight and keep it off.
  • Privacy. Some patients prefer the discretion of a telehealth visit over visiting a weight loss clinic in person. Your consultations are private and confidential.
  • Same clinical quality. FormBlends physicians provide the same thorough evaluation and personalized care you would expect from an in-person visit. The only difference is the delivery method.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is telehealth weight loss as effective as in-person programs?

Research suggests that telehealth-delivered weight loss programs can produce outcomes comparable to in-person programs, particularly when they include physician oversight and prescription medications. The key factor is consistent care, which telehealth makes easier for many patients.

What technology do I need for telehealth visits?

You need a smartphone, tablet, or computer with a camera and microphone, plus a stable internet connection. Our platform works on most modern devices and does not require special software downloads.

How often will I have follow-up appointments?

Follow-up frequency depends on your treatment plan, but most patients have check-ins every four to six weeks. Your physician may schedule more frequent visits during the initial dosing period.

What if the medication does not work for me?

Not every patient responds the same way to every medication. If your current treatment is not producing expected results, your physician can adjust the dose, switch medications, or explore alternative approaches. That is the value of ongoing physician supervision.

Can I cancel my program at any time?

Yes. FormBlends does not lock you into long-term contracts. You can discontinue your program at any time, though we recommend discussing any changes with your physician first to ensure a safe transition.
