What Are Anti-Aging Peptides?

Peptides are short chains of amino acids that act as signaling molecules in the body. Certain peptides have been studied for their role in stimulating collagen production, supporting cellular repair, improving sleep quality, and enhancing growth hormone output. These functions are closely tied to the biological processes that slow down with age.

Anti-aging peptide therapy does not reverse aging. It supports the body's natural repair and regeneration processes, helping patients maintain energy, skin elasticity, and recovery capacity as they get older.
