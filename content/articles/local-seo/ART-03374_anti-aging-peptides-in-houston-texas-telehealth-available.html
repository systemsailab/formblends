How Anti-Aging Peptides Work

Peptides are short chains of amino acids that serve as chemical messengers in your body. They signal your cells to perform specific functions, from building collagen to releasing growth hormone. Your body produces these peptides naturally, but production slows as you age. Anti-aging peptide therapy supplements those declining signals to support cellular repair, skin health, and metabolic function. {{CITE:Peptide signaling and age-related decline, Journal of Clinical Endocrinology and Metabolism}}
