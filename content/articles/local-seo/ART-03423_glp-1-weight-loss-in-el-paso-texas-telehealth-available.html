Who Qualifies for GLP-1 Weight Loss Treatment?

GLP-1 medications are prescribed to adults who meet established clinical criteria:

  • BMI of 30 or greater, or BMI of 27 or greater with at least one weight-related condition (hypertension, type 2 diabetes, dyslipidemia, or obstructive sleep apnea)
  • Previous attempts at weight loss through diet and exercise that did not produce lasting results
  • No contraindications including personal or family history of medullary thyroid carcinoma, MEN2 syndrome, or active pancreatitis

Your FormBlends physician makes the final determination during your consultation. We do not prescribe GLP-1 medications to patients who do not meet clinical thresholds.
