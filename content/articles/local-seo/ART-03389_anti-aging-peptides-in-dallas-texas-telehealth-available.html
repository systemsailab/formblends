What Are Anti-Aging Peptides?

Peptides are naturally occurring compounds made up of amino acids. Your body produces them on its own, but production tends to decline as you get older . Anti-aging peptide therapy works by supplementing specific peptides that support processes linked to youthful function.

Some of the key benefits associated with anti-aging peptides include:

  • Improved skin elasticity and texture
  • Enhanced muscle recovery and lean body composition
  • Better sleep quality and deeper restorative rest
  • Support for immune function and reduced inflammation
  • Increased energy and mental clarity

These are not miracle cures. They are targeted therapies backed by growing clinical research, prescribed and monitored by licensed medical professionals.
