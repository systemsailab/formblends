Eligibility for Weight Loss Injections

GLP-1 weight loss injections are prescription medications with specific eligibility guidelines:

  • BMI of 30 or greater
  • BMI of 27 or greater with one or more weight-related conditions such as high blood pressure, type 2 diabetes, or high cholesterol
  • A history of attempting weight loss through diet and exercise without sustained results
  • No current pregnancy or plans to conceive during treatment
  • No personal or family history of medullary thyroid carcinoma or MEN2

Your provider will confirm your eligibility during your telehealth consultation. We take candidacy seriously and only prescribe when it is medically appropriate.
