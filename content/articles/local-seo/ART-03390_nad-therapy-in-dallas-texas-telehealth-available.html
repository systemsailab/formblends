Nicotinamide adenine dinucleotide, or NAD+, is a coenzyme found in every living cell. It is essential for converting food into energy, maintaining DNA integrity, and regulating your body's stress response . The problem is that your NAD+ levels drop steadily as you age, contributing to fatigue, cognitive decline, and slower recovery .

Why NAD+ Therapy Matters

Think of NAD+ as fuel for your cells. When levels are high, your cells function efficiently. When they drop, things start to slow down. Research has connected declining NAD+ levels to a wide range of age-related changes, including:

  • Reduced mental sharpness and focus
  • Lower energy throughout the day
  • Slower muscle recovery after physical activity
  • Weakened immune response
  • Accelerated visible aging

NAD+ therapy aims to replenish these levels, giving your cells the resources they need to perform at their best. It is not about turning back the clock. It is about giving your body what it needs to function optimally right now.
