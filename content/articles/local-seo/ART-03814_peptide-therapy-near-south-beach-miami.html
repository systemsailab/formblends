Understanding Peptide Therapy

Peptides are naturally occurring chains of amino acids that serve as chemical messengers in the body . They play roles in nearly every biological process, from hormone regulation and immune response to tissue repair and metabolism. Peptide therapy uses specific synthetic or bioidentical peptides to enhance or restore these natural functions.

Different peptide protocols address different goals. Some are designed to stimulate growth hormone release for improved lean muscle and fat loss. Others target gut health, inflammation, or cellular recovery. At Form Blends, your provider selects a protocol based on a detailed review of your health history and what you want to achieve.
