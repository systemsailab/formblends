Frequently Asked Questions

Are weight loss injections painful?

Most patients describe the injection as a minor pinch. The needle is very small, and the injection goes just under the skin (subcutaneous). Many people get comfortable with the process after just one or two doses.

How often do I need to inject?

Both semaglutide and tirzepatide are given once per week. You choose the day that works best for your schedule, and you can do it at home in about 30 seconds.

What are the most common side effects of weight loss injections?

Nausea is the most frequently reported side effect, particularly during the early weeks or after a dose increase. Other possible effects include mild constipation, diarrhea, or stomach discomfort. These usually improve as your body adjusts .

Can I take weight loss injections if I am on other medications?

Possibly, but your physician needs to review your full medication list first. Certain drugs, especially insulin and other diabetes medications, may require dose adjustments when starting a GLP-1 injection. Your Form Blends doctor will evaluate any potential interactions during your consultation.
