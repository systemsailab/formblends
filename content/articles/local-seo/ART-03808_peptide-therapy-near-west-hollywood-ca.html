What Is Peptide Therapy?

Peptides are short chains of amino acids that function as signaling molecules throughout the body . Your body naturally produces thousands of different peptides, each with specific roles in processes like hormone regulation, immune response, tissue repair, and metabolism. As we age or face ongoing physical demands, some of these peptide pathways become less efficient.

Peptide therapy involves administering targeted peptides to support or restore specific biological functions. Depending on the protocol, peptide therapy may help with lean muscle development, fat metabolism, post-exercise recovery, sleep quality, cognitive clarity, and gut health .
