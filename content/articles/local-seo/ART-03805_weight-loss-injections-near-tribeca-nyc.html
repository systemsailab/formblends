Are You a Candidate for Weight Loss Injections?

Weight loss injections are appropriate for adults who meet specific medical guidelines. You may be eligible if:

  • Your BMI is at or above 30, or at or above 27 with a weight-related health condition
  • You have tried to manage your weight through lifestyle modifications without lasting success
  • You are in overall stable health and not currently pregnant or planning pregnancy
  • You have no contraindications such as a history of medullary thyroid carcinoma, pancreatitis, or MEN2 syndrome

Your Form Blends provider conducts a thorough evaluation before writing any prescription. Our goal is to make sure every patient receiving weight loss injections is doing so safely and with realistic expectations.
