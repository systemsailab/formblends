Frequently Asked Questions

Is medical weight loss just about taking medication?

No. While prescription medications are often a core component, medical weight loss also includes physician guidance on nutrition, activity, and behavioral strategies. The medication helps reduce appetite and improve metabolic function, while lifestyle adjustments support long-term success.

How much weight can I expect to lose?

Results vary by individual. Patients on GLP-1 medications in clinical trials lost an average of 15% to 22% of their body weight, depending on the medication used. Your physician will set personalized goals based on your starting point and health profile. {{CITE:GLP-1 weight loss outcomes}}

Will I gain the weight back if I stop the medication?

Weight regain is possible after discontinuing medication, which is why FormBlends emphasizes building sustainable habits alongside pharmacotherapy. Your physician will work with you on a transition plan when the time comes to taper or discontinue treatment.
