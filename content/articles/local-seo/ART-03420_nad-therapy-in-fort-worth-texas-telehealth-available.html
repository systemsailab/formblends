Why Patients Choose NAD+ Therapy

Interest in NAD+ has grown rapidly as research uncovers its importance in aging and cellular health. Fort Worth patients who explore NAD+ therapy with FormBlends typically want to address one or more of the following:

  • Low energy and chronic fatigue. NAD+ fuels mitochondrial function, the process your cells use to convert nutrients into usable energy. Declining NAD+ levels can leave you feeling drained even after a full night of sleep.
  • Cognitive performance. NAD+ supports neuronal health and has been linked to improved focus, memory, and mental clarity in emerging research.
  • Recovery from physical activity. Athletes and active adults may benefit from NAD+ supplementation to support faster recovery between workouts.
  • Longevity and preventive health. NAD+ activates sirtuins, a group of proteins involved in DNA repair and stress resistance, making it a cornerstone of many longevity-focused wellness plans.

Frequently Asked Questions

What delivery methods are available for NAD+?

FormBlends physicians may prescribe NAD+ in subcutaneous injection form, nasal spray, or oral supplement, depending on your clinical needs and preferences. Your provider will recommend the best option during your consultation.

Are there side effects with NAD+ therapy?

NAD+ therapy is generally well tolerated. Some patients experience mild flushing, warmth, or brief discomfort at the injection site. These effects typically resolve quickly and tend to diminish with continued use.

How long do I need to take NAD+?

Is NAD+ therapy backed by science?
