When to See a Weight Loss Doctor

Consider consulting a weight loss doctor if you experience any of the following:

  • You have tried multiple diets without sustained results
  • Your BMI is 27 or above with a weight-related health condition, or 30 or above
  • You are gaining weight despite maintaining healthy habits
  • Your primary care doctor has suggested you consider medication for weight management
  • You are concerned about the health risks associated with excess weight, such as diabetes, heart disease, or joint problems
