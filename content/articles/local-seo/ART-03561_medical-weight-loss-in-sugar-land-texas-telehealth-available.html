Frequently Asked Questions

How is medical weight loss different from a diet program?

Medical weight loss involves prescription medications prescribed by a physician, not meal plans or supplements. The medications target hormonal pathways that regulate appetite and metabolism.

Do I need to change my diet while on GLP-1 medication?

While the medication reduces appetite on its own, combining it with balanced nutrition and regular activity improves outcomes. Your physician may offer general dietary guidance during your consultations.

How long does medical weight loss treatment last?
