How Our Online Clinic Works

FormBlends operates like a traditional weight loss clinic, with one important difference: everything happens online. Here is the process:

  1. Register and complete your intake. Create your account and fill out a comprehensive health questionnaire. This gives your physician the context needed for a productive consultation.
  2. Meet your physician by video. A licensed provider conducts your evaluation over a secure video call. Expect a thorough discussion about your weight, health conditions, past treatment attempts, and goals.
  3. Receive your prescription. If you qualify for GLP-1 medication, your physician writes a prescription that is filled by a licensed pharmacy. Medication ships directly to your El Paso address.
  4. Stay connected through follow-ups. Scheduled check-ins give you direct access to your provider. These visits are used to track your weight loss, manage any side effects, and adjust your treatment plan.

Frequently Asked Questions

How is an online weight loss clinic different from a weight loss app?

Weight loss apps typically offer tracking tools, meal plans, and community support. An online weight loss clinic like FormBlends provides licensed physician care, prescription medications, and clinical monitoring. The distinction is medical oversight, which is what enables access to proven GLP-1 medications.

Is my health information secure?

Yes. FormBlends uses HIPAA-compliant technology for all consultations, health records, and communications. Your personal health information is protected to the same standard as any in-person medical practice.

What if I am not comfortable with self-injection?

Many patients feel nervous about self-injection at first. Your physician will walk you through the process during your consultation, and you will receive written instructions with your medication. Most patients find the injection quick and nearly painless after their first attempt.

Can I see the same doctor for each visit?

We strive to pair you with the same provider throughout your treatment for continuity of care. This helps your physician track your progress over time and build a relationship with you as a patient.

Do you serve areas outside El Paso?

Yes. FormBlends is available to all Texas residents. Whether you are in El Paso, the Lower Valley, Anthony, or anywhere else in the state, our online clinic is accessible to you.
