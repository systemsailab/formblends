Understanding GLP-1 Medications

GLP-1 stands for glucagon-like peptide-1, a hormone produced naturally in the gut after eating. It signals the brain to reduce appetite and helps regulate blood sugar. GLP-1 receptor agonist medications amplify this effect, producing sustained appetite reduction and meaningful weight loss.
