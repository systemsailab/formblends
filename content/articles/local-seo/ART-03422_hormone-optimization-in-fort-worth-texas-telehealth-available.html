Candidacy and Clinical Standards

Not every patient who feels tired needs hormone therapy. Our physicians follow a rigorous evaluation process to ensure that treatment is both appropriate and safe. To qualify, you generally need:

  • Symptoms consistent with hormonal deficiency or imbalance
  • Lab work confirming suboptimal or deficient hormone levels
  • No absolute contraindications to hormone therapy (e.g., certain cancers, active blood clots)

If your evaluation suggests that lifestyle modifications, nutritional support, or other approaches are better suited to your situation, your provider will recommend those instead. We only prescribe hormones when the clinical picture clearly supports it.
