What Are Weight Loss Injections?

Weight loss injections prescribed by Form Blends are GLP-1 receptor agonists administered as a once-weekly subcutaneous injection. These medications work by mimicking a gut hormone that controls appetite and blood sugar regulation. The result is reduced hunger, smaller portions, and steady weight loss over time.

These are not vitamin shots or B12 injections. GLP-1 weight loss injections are prescription medications that require physician evaluation and ongoing monitoring.
