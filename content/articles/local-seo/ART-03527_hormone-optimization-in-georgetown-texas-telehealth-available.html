What Is Hormone Optimization?

Hormone optimization is a medical approach to identifying and addressing hormonal imbalances that affect energy, body composition, mood, sleep, libido, and overall well-being. As men and women age, key hormones such as testosterone, growth hormone, thyroid hormones, and others naturally decline, leading to symptoms that many people accept as an inevitable part of aging. {{CITE:age-related hormone decline}}

Through targeted therapies, including peptides and other physician-prescribed treatments, hormone optimization aims to restore hormonal levels to a range that supports optimal function. This is not about achieving artificially high levels but about bringing your body back into a balanced state. {{MEDICAL_REVIEW:hormone optimization philosophy}}
