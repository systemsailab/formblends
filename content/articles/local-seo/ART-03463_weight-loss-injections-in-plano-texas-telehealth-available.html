Frequently Asked Questions

Do weight loss injections hurt?

Most patients describe a brief pinch. The needles used for subcutaneous injection are very small, similar to what diabetic patients use for insulin. Any discomfort is minimal and lasts only a moment.

Where do I inject the medication?

Subcutaneous injections are typically given in the abdomen, front of the thigh, or upper arm. Your physician and our instructional materials guide you on proper injection technique and site rotation {{CITE:subcutaneous injection technique and site rotation best practices}}.

How quickly do weight loss injections start working?

Most patients notice reduced appetite within the first week or two. Measurable weight loss typically becomes apparent within the first month. Significant results build over three to six months of consistent use.

Can I stop the injections once I reach my goal weight?

Your physician will help you plan for maintenance once you reach your target. Some patients transition to a lower maintenance dose, while others taper off entirely.
