Who Qualifies for Medical Weight Loss

Form Blends' medical weight loss program is designed for adults who meet clinical criteria for pharmacological intervention:

  • BMI of 30 or above
  • BMI of 27 or above with a weight-related health condition
  • Previous unsuccessful attempts at sustained weight loss

Certain conditions may prevent eligibility, including pregnancy, a history of medullary thyroid cancer, or MEN2 syndrome. Your physician will assess all factors during your initial consultation.
