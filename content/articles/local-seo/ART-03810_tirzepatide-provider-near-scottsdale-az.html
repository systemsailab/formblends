Who Qualifies for Tirzepatide?

Tirzepatide is a prescription medication with specific eligibility requirements. You may be a candidate if:

  • Your BMI is 30 or higher, placing you in the clinical category of obesity
  • Your BMI is 27 or higher and you have a weight-related comorbidity such as type 2 diabetes, hypertension, or dyslipidemia
  • You have attempted weight loss through non-pharmaceutical means without achieving lasting results
  • You are not pregnant, nursing, or planning pregnancy
  • You have no history of medullary thyroid carcinoma or MEN2 syndrome
