What the Science Says About GLP-1 Medications

GLP-1 receptor agonists have been the subject of extensive clinical research. The data consistently shows that these medications produce meaningful, sustained weight loss that surpasses what most people can achieve through diet and exercise alone.

Semaglutide trials showed average weight loss around 15% of body weight. Tirzepatide studies have shown results exceeding 20% in many participants. To put that in perspective, someone starting at 250 pounds could expect to lose 37 to 50 pounds or more over the course of treatment. These are not marginal improvements. Side effects are generally manageable. Nausea is the most commonly reported issue, particularly during the early weeks as your dose increases. Most patients find it mild and temporary. Your provider will use a gradual titration schedule to minimize discomfort and will adjust your plan if needed.
