Longevity Supplements Stack: Protocol 2026

March 6, 2026

The 2026 longevity supplement protocol reflects new clinical data on NAD+ precursors, refined senolytic dosing strategies, updated perspectives on resveratrol, and emerging compounds like urolithin A and spermidine that have moved from animal research into human trials. This year's protocol emphasizes cycling strategies, combination synergies, and biomarker-driven adjustments over static daily stacking.

What Changed From 2025 to 2026

Several developments in late 2025 and early 2026 have shifted longevity supplement recommendations:

  • NMN human trial data matured: Multiple completed trials confirmed NMN raises NAD+ levels in humans and improves insulin sensitivity and aerobic capacity in older adults. Dosing consensus has settled around 500 to 1,000 mg daily.
  • Fisetin human trials published: Early human data on fisetin's senolytic potential has arrived, showing good tolerability and preliminary evidence of senescent cell clearance at higher doses.
  • Resveratrol debate continues: Some longevity researchers have deprioritized resveratrol due to mixed human data and potential estrogenic effects. Others maintain it synergizes with NAD+ precursors. The 2026 protocol positions it as optional.
  • Urolithin A gained traction: New trials demonstrated improved mitochondrial function and muscle endurance in older adults.
  • Cycling protocols adopted: The longevity community has moved toward cycling certain compounds (senolytics, adaptogens) rather than taking everything daily, reflecting how these compounds work mechanistically.

The 2026 Cycling Protocols

Not everything should be taken daily. These compounds work best when cycled, either because they trigger acute responses that lose efficacy with constant use, or because their mechanisms benefit from periodic rather than continuous exposure.

Senolytic Protocol (2 days per month)

Senolytic compounds clear senescent (zombie) cells. These cells accumulate with age and secrete inflammatory signals (the senescence-associated secretory phenotype, or SASP) that damage surrounding tissue. Senolytics work acutely, so daily dosing is unnecessary and may reduce selectivity.

  • Fisetin: 1,000 to 1,500 mg for 2 consecutive days
  • Quercetin: 1,000 mg for 2 consecutive days (taken alongside fisetin)

Emerging Additions for 2026

Urolithin A

Dose: 500 mg daily

Mechanism: Promotes mitophagy, the selective recycling of dysfunctional mitochondria. This is distinct from general autophagy. By clearing damaged mitochondria and stimulating production of new ones, urolithin A improves mitochondrial quality. Clinical trials in older adults showed improved muscle endurance and biomarkers of mitochondrial health.

Urolithin A is naturally produced by gut bacteria from ellagic acid found in pomegranates and berries. However, only about 40% of people have the microbiome composition to produce adequate amounts, making supplementation valuable for the majority. Contact provider for current pricing

Alpha-Ketoglutarate (CaAKG)

Dose: 1,000 mg daily (calcium AKG form)

Mechanism: Krebs cycle intermediate that declines with age. Animal studies showed compressed morbidity (healthier aging) and lifespan extension. What to Remove From Old Protocols

Longevity science evolves, and some previously popular compounds deserve reassessment:

  • High-dose antioxidant megadosing: Evidence suggests that moderate ROS signaling is beneficial (hormesis). Megadosing vitamins C and E may blunt exercise adaptations and interfere with beneficial stress responses.
  • Isolated beta-carotene: Supplementation in smokers increased lung cancer risk. Get carotenoids from food instead.
  • Daily low-dose rapamycin without medical supervision: While rapamycin is the most robust lifespan-extending compound in animal models, self-prescribing carries immune suppression risks. Use only under physician guidance.

Biomarker-Driven Adjustments

Test every 3 to 6 months and adjust based on results:

  • hs-CRP above 1.0 mg/L: Increase omega-3 dose, add curcumin, investigate root cause of inflammation.
  • Vitamin D below 50 ng/mL: Increase D3 dose by 2,000 IU and retest in 8 weeks.
  • Omega-3 index below 8%: Increase EPA/DHA to 3 to 4 grams daily.
  • Fasting insulin above 5 uIU/mL: Focus on metabolic interventions (exercise, time-restricted eating, berberine consideration).
  • Biological age above chronological age: Review all lifestyle factors, increase exercise, and consider adding AKG or optimizing NAD+ support.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the most important update to the longevity stack for 2026?
The shift toward cycling protocols rather than daily dosing for senolytics and certain anti-inflammatory compounds. This approach better matches how these compounds work mechanistically and reduces unnecessary chronic exposure.
Should I take NMN or NR in 2026?
Either is effective. NMN has more recent human trial data and appears to be slightly more efficient at raising NAD+ levels per milligram, but NR has a longer safety track record. Choose based on availability, cost, and personal response.
Is the full protocol necessary for longevity benefits?
No. The foundation stack (Tier 1) delivers the majority of the benefit. Each additional tier offers diminishing returns at increasing cost. Prioritize lifestyle factors (sleep, exercise, nutrition, stress management) over adding more pills. biohacking beginners guide complete guide
How do I know if my longevity supplements are working?
Track biomarkers every 3 to 6 months: hs-CRP, omega-3 index, vitamin D, fasting insulin, and HbA1c. Consider biological age testing (epigenetic clocks) annually. Subjective improvements in energy, sleep quality, and recovery are also valuable signals.
Can I take longevity supplements alongside prescription medications?
Some supplements interact with medications. Omega-3s can potentiate blood thinners. Curcumin interacts with certain drugs through CYP enzyme modulation. NMN and NR interactions are less well-characterized. Always consult your prescribing physician before adding supplements to a medication regimen.
