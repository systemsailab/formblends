Challenges Unique to 100-Pound Losses

Metabolic Resistance

The body aggressively defends against extreme weight loss through hormonal changes that increase hunger and reduce energy expenditure. Semaglutide counteracts much of this resistance, which is one of its key advantages over traditional dieting, but some adaptation is inevitable. {{CITE:Hormonal resistance to major weight loss}}

Loose Skin

Significant loose skin is expected with 100 pounds of loss. It can cause physical discomfort, skin infections, and cosmetic concerns. Many patients pursue skin removal surgery after weight stabilization. Planning for this possibility helps set realistic expectations.

Social and Identity Shifts

Losing 100 pounds makes you physically unrecognizable to some people. This can be exhilarating but also disorienting. Relationships built around shared eating habits may change. Navigating these shifts with awareness and support is important for long-term well-being.
