Eligibility Requirements

To qualify for a tirzepatide prescription, you generally need to meet one of the following criteria:

  • BMI of 30 or higher (classified as obesity)
  • BMI of 27 or higher with at least one weight-related condition such as type 2 diabetes, high blood pressure, high cholesterol, or sleep apnea

Who Should NOT Take Tirzepatide

Tirzepatide is not appropriate for everyone. Your provider will screen for:

  • Personal or family history of medullary thyroid carcinoma
  • Multiple endocrine neoplasia syndrome type 2 (MEN 2)
  • History of pancreatitis
  • Pregnancy or plans to become pregnant
  • Severe gastrointestinal disease
  • Known allergy to tirzepatide or its components
