Frequently Asked Questions

What if I miss a dose?

If you miss a dose, take it as soon as you remember if it is within a reasonable window. If your next scheduled dose is approaching, skip the missed dose and resume your regular schedule. Never double up. Contact your physician if you are unsure.

Can I stay at a lower dose if it is working?

Yes. If you are achieving good results with tolerable side effects at a lower dose, your physician may recommend staying there rather than increasing. The goal is the most effective dose with the fewest side effects, not necessarily the highest dose available.

How long does the titration period last?

Titration typically takes several weeks to a few months depending on the specific medication and your response. Your physician controls the pace based on your tolerance and results.
