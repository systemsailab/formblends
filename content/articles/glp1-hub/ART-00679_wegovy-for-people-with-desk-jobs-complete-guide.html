Getting Started with Wegovy

Your Workday on Wegovy

Morning

Eat a protein-forward breakfast even if you are not very hungry. Two eggs and toast, Greek yogurt with fruit, or a protein shake. This sets up stable blood sugar for your morning and prevents the mid-morning slump that leads to breakroom grazing.

Midday

Lunch will naturally be smaller. A palm-sized portion of protein, a generous serving of vegetables, and a moderate portion of complex carbs is a solid template. Bring your lunch or pre-select a restaurant order to avoid impulsive choices when hunger is low and decision fatigue is high.

Afternoon

This is where Wegovy shines. The afternoon snack craving that derails most desk worker diets simply does not show up with the same force. If you do get hungry, a small protein snack (cheese stick, handful of almonds, protein bar) is enough. Many Wegovy users report that the afternoon passes without a single food thought.

Evening

Dinner portions naturally shrink on Wegovy. Focus on protein and vegetables. The reduced appetite often means you stop eating earlier in the evening, which itself has metabolic benefits for blood sugar and sleep quality.

Nutrition Priorities on Wegovy

Protein

Aim for 0.7 to 1.0 grams per pound of body weight daily. This preserves lean mass during weight loss, which is especially important for desk workers who already have lower muscle mass from inactivity. Protein also keeps you satisfied longer, complementing Wegovy's appetite effects.

Hydration

Constipation is the side effect most likely to persist beyond the initial adjustment. Drink 64 to 80 ounces of water throughout the workday. Keep a visible water bottle on your desk as a reminder.

Fiber

Fruits, vegetables, and whole grains support digestive health on Wegovy. However, avoid loading up on fiber right before meals if nausea is an issue, as high-fiber foods take longer to digest with slowed gastric emptying.

What to Avoid

High-fat, greasy meals are harder to tolerate on Wegovy. Fast food, fried foods, and rich restaurant meals may cause nausea or discomfort. This is actually a helpful guardrail: the foods that caused weight gain in the first place are now unpleasant to eat.

Adding Movement (Without a Gym Membership)

Wegovy does not require exercise to work, but small additions make a real difference:

  • Walking: 7,000 to 10,000 steps daily. Take phone calls while walking. Use a treadmill desk if available. Walk to a farther coffee shop for your morning cup.
  • Standing desk rotation: Alternate between sitting and standing every 30 to 60 minutes.
  • Bodyweight exercises: Squats, push-ups, and planks at home. Ten minutes in the morning, three times per week, protects muscle mass.
  • Post-lunch walk: Even ten minutes improves post-meal blood sugar handling and makes the afternoon more productive.

Side Effects at the Office

  • Nausea: Usually mild. Keep ginger chews or crackers at your desk. Eat slowly. Avoid large, heavy meals. Most nausea resolves within one to two weeks of each dose increase.
  • Constipation: Water and fiber are your primary tools. A gentle magnesium supplement before bed can also help.
  • Fatigue: If you feel unusually tired, check your calorie intake. Under-eating is the most common cause of fatigue on Wegovy, not the medication itself.
  • Injection site reactions: Rare and minor. Rotate injection sites (abdomen, thigh, upper arm) weekly.
