Wegovy Results After 2 Weeks: What to Expect

Wegovy results after 2 weeks are all about adjusting, not about arriving. You have completed two injections at the 0.25 mg dose, and your body is acclimating to semaglutide. Side effects from the first week have likely improved, appetite changes are becoming more predictable, and you may have lost a few pounds. But make no mistake: the 0.25 mg phase is preparation, not treatment. Here is exactly where you should be.

Appetite Changes at Week 2

By week 2, patients generally fall into one of three categories:

Group 1: Clear Appetite Reduction (About 40%)

You feel noticeably less hungry between meals. Portions at meals are naturally smaller. You may catch yourself thinking, "I used to eat way more than this." Snacking urges have decreased.

Group 2: Subtle Changes (About 35%)

You feel a little different around food but cannot pinpoint exactly what changed. Meals are slightly more satisfying. You might eat a bit less without actively trying. The changes are real but quiet.

Group 3: No Noticeable Change (About 25%)

You feel the same as before starting Wegovy. Your appetite, portions, and cravings are unchanged. This is completely fine at 0.25 mg. Many patients in this group go on to have excellent results at higher doses.

All three groups can achieve strong long-term weight loss. Early appetite response does not predict final outcomes. Wegovy weight loss expectations

Preparing for What Comes Next

You have 2 more weeks at 0.25 mg before increasing to 0.5 mg. Use this time productively:

  • Master your protein routine: Build consistent meals with 25-30g protein per meal. This becomes critical at higher doses when you eat less
  • Stock your kitchen: Greek yogurt, chicken, eggs, fish, protein powder, vegetables, whole grains
  • Establish an exercise pattern: Regular walking plus 2-3 resistance training sessions per week
  • Start a simple tracking system: Weekly weight, daily protein intake, and a brief note on appetite/energy levels
  • Prepare mentally: The 0.5 mg dose may bring a brief return of mild nausea. This is normal and usually passes within 3-5 days

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I stay at 0.25 mg longer if I am nervous about increasing?

Your provider can extend the introductory period if needed, but there is little benefit to staying at 0.25 mg beyond 4-6 weeks since it is sub-therapeutic. Most patients tolerate the increase to 0.5 mg well. Wegovy dose escalation

Is it normal to be more constipated at week 2?

Yes. Semaglutide slows gastric motility, and this effect accumulates. Increase water intake, eat fiber-rich foods, and consider a daily magnesium supplement. If constipation is severe, talk to your provider about a stool softener.

Should I weigh myself more often to track progress?

Once per week is ideal. Daily weighing at this early stage will only show you normal fluid fluctuations that feel like noise. A consistent weekly weigh-in (same day, same time, same conditions) gives you the clearest trend line.

Is 2 weeks long enough to have side effects from Wegovy?

Most side effects at 0.25 mg appear in week 1 and resolve by week 2. New side effects appearing for the first time in week 2 are uncommon. If they do occur, report them to your provider.

Stay the Course

Two weeks on Wegovy is barely a start. The medication is designed to unfold over months, and the patients who get the best results are the ones who stay consistent through these early, quiet weeks. Every day at the starting dose is preparing your body for the therapeutic doses ahead.
