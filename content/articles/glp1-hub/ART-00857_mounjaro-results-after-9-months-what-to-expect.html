The Diabetes Story at Nine Months

For Mounjaro patients with type 2 diabetes, the nine-month results are often the most clinically significant aspect of treatment {{MEDICAL_REVIEW:tirzepatide diabetes outcomes at 9 months}}:

  • A1C normalization: Many patients achieve A1C below 5.7%, which is outside the diabetic and even prediabetic range.
  • Insulin independence: Patients who started on insulin have often been able to discontinue it entirely.
  • Oral medication reduction: Metformin, sulfonylureas, and other oral diabetes medications are frequently reduced or stopped.
  • Cardiovascular risk reduction: The combined effect of weight loss, improved glucose, and better lipids dramatically lowers heart disease risk.
  • Kidney protection: Improved blood sugar and blood pressure protect kidney function.

These outcomes represent a fundamental shift in how type 2 diabetes can be managed. Mounjaro does not just treat the symptoms of diabetes; it addresses the underlying insulin resistance and excess weight that drive the disease.
