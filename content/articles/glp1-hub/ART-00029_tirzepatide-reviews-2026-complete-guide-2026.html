How Tirzepatide Works

Understanding how tirzepatide works helps explain why the reviews are so positive. The medication activates two hormone receptors:

  • GLP-1 receptors: Slow gastric emptying, increase insulin secretion, and reduce appetite signals in the brain.
  • GIP receptors: Enhance fat metabolism, improve insulin sensitivity, and contribute to additional weight loss beyond what GLP-1 alone can achieve.

This dual action is why tirzepatide consistently outperforms single-target GLP-1 medications in head-to-head comparisons.
