Wegovy in the College Environment

Dining Hall Strategy

Wegovy does not require a special diet, but it works best when you make reasonable food choices. Here is a realistic dining hall approach:

  • Breakfast: Scrambled eggs or omelet from the hot station, fruit, and coffee. Skip the sugary cereal and pastry table.
  • Lunch: Grilled protein (chicken, fish, or turkey) from the entree station, salad with olive oil dressing, and one carb serving.
  • Dinner: Same protein-first approach. The beauty of Wegovy is that one plate genuinely satisfies you.
  • Late night: If you study late and need fuel, Greek yogurt or a protein bar from your room beats a pizza order every time. On Wegovy, the late-night craving that used to feel irresistible becomes a mild suggestion you can easily ignore.

The Social Scene

Wegovy does not prevent you from having a social life. You still go to parties, dinners, and campus events. You just eat less at them. Your friends will not notice you had one slice instead of three unless you make it a topic of conversation. Some students find it helpful to have one trusted friend who knows about their medication for accountability and support.

Storage in Your Dorm

Wegovy pens are single-use and pre-filled. Store unused pens in your mini-fridge. If your dorm does not have a fridge, talk to your RA about medical storage options or invest in a small personal cooler with ice packs. Single-dose pens can be kept at room temperature for up to 28 days, which covers most dorm situations.

Exercise Pairing

Your tuition includes access to the campus recreation center. Take advantage of it. A simple routine of three 30-minute resistance training sessions per week preserves muscle mass during weight loss and improves body composition beyond what medication alone achieves. Walking to class (instead of taking the campus shuttle) adds meaningful daily movement.

Handling Side Effects in College

  • Nausea: Most common during the first four weeks. Eat small meals. Avoid the fried food station. Ginger tea from the campus cafe helps. If nausea hits during a lecture, sipping water and having a plain cracker usually settles it.
  • Constipation: Drink more water than you think you need. The dining hall salad bar (fiber from vegetables) and walking between classes help.
  • Fatigue: Usually mild and temporary. If it interferes with studying, make sure you are eating enough protein (you may need to eat even when not hungry).
  • Injection site soreness: Minor and brief. Rotate between abdomen and thigh. Do not inject where your waistband sits if it causes irritation.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use Wegovy if I am on my parents' insurance?

Yes. If you are covered under a parent's plan, Wegovy can be prescribed and billed to that insurance. A prior authorization may be required. Your telehealth provider handles the paperwork. Be aware that your parents may see the insurance claim, so discuss with them first if privacy is a concern.

Will Wegovy show up on a drug test?

No. Semaglutide is not a controlled substance and is not included on standard drug screening panels. It will not affect employment screenings, athletic testing, or any other standard drug test.

What if I go abroad for a semester?

Plan ahead. Bring enough medication for the entire semester (approximately 16 to 20 pens). Store them in your dorm or apartment fridge abroad. Your telehealth provider can do video follow-ups from overseas, though prescriptions can only be filled at US pharmacies. {{MEDICAL_REVIEW:Wegovy supply planning for study abroad}}

Can I take Wegovy with ADHD medication?

Stimulant ADHD medications (Adderall, Vyvanse, Ritalin) are common on college campuses. There are no direct pharmacological interactions with Wegovy, but both medication types can suppress appetite. Your provider needs to know about all medications you take to ensure you are eating enough to stay healthy and maintain muscle mass. {{MEDICAL_REVIEW:Wegovy and stimulant ADHD medication coadministration}}

How long should a college student take Wegovy?

Treatment duration varies. Many patients use Wegovy for 12 to 24 months to reach their target weight, then work with their provider on a maintenance plan (which may include continued medication at a lower dose or a transition off). Starting in college gives you the advantage of establishing healthy habits and a lower weight before entering the workforce.
