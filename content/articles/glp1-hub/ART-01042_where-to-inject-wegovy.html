Where to Inject Wegovy?

Wegovy is injected subcutaneously into the abdomen, the front of the upper thigh, or the back of the upper arm. These are the same three injection sites used for all subcutaneous semaglutide products. The Wegovy pen is a single-dose device with a hidden needle, making it straightforward to use. Rotate your injection site weekly to prevent tissue damage and maintain consistent absorption.

Detailed Answer: Wegovy Injection Sites and the Single-Dose Pen

Wegovy (semaglutide 2.4 mg) is FDA-approved for chronic weight management and uses a unique single-dose, disposable pen {{CITE:Wegovy pen prescribing information}}. Unlike the Ozempic multi-dose pen, the Wegovy pen comes pre-loaded with your exact weekly dose and has a hidden needle that you never see or handle.

Abdomen

The abdomen remains the most popular injection site for Wegovy patients. Inject into the fatty tissue at least two inches from the belly button. The lower abdomen offers the most consistent subcutaneous fat in most body types. This site gives you a clear view of what you are doing, which is especially helpful if you are new to self-injection.

Upper Thigh

The front and outer area of the upper thigh, in the middle section between hip and knee, is a reliable alternative. The thigh is easy to access while seated and offers a good amount of subcutaneous tissue. Some patients alternate between abdomen and thigh on a weekly basis for effective rotation.

Upper Arm

The back of the upper arm works well if someone can assist you. The hidden needle design of the Wegovy pen makes this site slightly easier than with traditional needle-and-syringe setups, but reaching the back of your own arm can still be awkward. If you regularly use this site, consider having a partner help.

What You Need to Know: Using the Wegovy Pen

How the Wegovy Pen Works

The Wegovy pen is designed for simplicity. Remove the pen cap to reveal the injection window. Place the pen flat against your cleaned skin at the chosen site. Press and hold the injection button. You will hear two clicks: the first indicates the injection has started, and the second means it is complete. The process takes about 5 to 10 seconds. A yellow bar will appear in the window to confirm the dose was delivered.

No Needle Handling Required

One of the advantages of the Wegovy pen is that the needle is completely hidden. You never attach, see, or remove a needle. This makes the experience less intimidating for patients who are uncomfortable with needles. After use, the needle retracts automatically, and you dispose of the entire pen in a sharps container.

Site Rotation Strategy

Since Wegovy is a weekly injection, you have seven days between doses. A simple rotation plan is to alternate between your left and right sides of the abdomen, then move to the thighs, creating a four-week cycle. Keep each injection at least one to two inches from the previous spot within any given area.

Storage and Handling

Keep unused Wegovy pens in the refrigerator at 36 to 46 degrees Fahrenheit. You can store a pen at room temperature (up to 86 degrees) for up to 28 days before use. Do not freeze Wegovy pens, and do not use a pen that has been frozen. Allow refrigerated pens to reach room temperature for about 30 minutes before injecting for a more comfortable experience {{CITE:Wegovy storage and handling instructions}}.

What to Do If the Pen Malfunctions

If the yellow confirmation bar does not appear or if the injection feels incomplete, do not attempt to use the same pen again. Contact your pharmacy or provider for a replacement. Do not try to administer a partial dose from a second pen without guidance from your healthcare team.

Related Questions

Is the Wegovy pen different from Ozempic?

Yes. Wegovy uses a single-dose pen with a hidden needle that you dispose of after each use. Ozempic uses a multi-dose pen where you attach needles and dial your dose. The injection sites and subcutaneous technique are the same for both {{INTERNAL_LINK:where to inject ozempic}}.

How do you rotate Wegovy injection sites?

Alternate between different spots within the same body area or switch body areas each week. A four-spot rotation on the abdomen (upper left, upper right, lower left, lower right) is a common approach. The key is keeping at least one to two inches between consecutive injection sites.

Does Wegovy injection site affect weight loss?

No. The injection site does not influence how effectively Wegovy works. All three approved sites deliver the medication reliably. Choose based on comfort and convenience, not based on weight loss expectations.

Can I inject Wegovy into my love handles?

The lateral abdomen (what some call the love handles area) is acceptable as long as you are injecting into subcutaneous tissue and staying within the abdominal zone. Avoid injecting too far toward the back or into areas where the skin is very tight over muscle.
