Common Side Effects

Most side effects are GI-related and tend to improve as your body adjusts:

  • Nausea (most common, usually mild)
  • Diarrhea or constipation
  • Bloating or gas
  • Decreased appetite (this is also the desired effect)
  • Headache during early weeks

Eating smaller meals, avoiding greasy foods, and staying hydrated can reduce discomfort during the adjustment period.

Lifestyle Tips for Men Over 40 on Wegovy

  • Lift weights: Resistance training is non-negotiable. It preserves muscle, supports bone density, and keeps your metabolism from dropping further as you lose weight.
