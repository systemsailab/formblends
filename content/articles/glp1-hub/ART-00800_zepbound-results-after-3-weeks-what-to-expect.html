Preparing for 5 mg: Your Transition Playbook

The Week Before

  • Stock your kitchen with easy, protein-rich options: rotisserie chicken, Greek yogurt, protein shakes, pre-cooked eggs
  • Have bland comfort foods available: crackers, broth, ginger chews, peppermint tea
  • Plan lighter meals for the 3-4 days after your first 5 mg injection
  • Do not start a new intense exercise program the same week as a dose increase

Injection Day (5 mg)

  • Take your injection at your usual time
  • Eat a light, protein-focused meal beforehand
  • Keep your schedule light for the next 24-48 hours in case of nausea

Days 1-4 After Increase

  • Eat small, frequent meals rather than 3 large ones
  • Stay very well hydrated (constipation often worsens temporarily)
  • Appetite will likely drop noticeably. This is the medication working. Make sure you still eat enough protein
  • Light exercise (walking) is fine. Save intense workouts for day 4+

Frequently Asked Questions

How much faster will I lose weight at 5 mg?

Most patients at 5 mg lose 0.5 to 1.5 lbs per week, compared to roughly 0.5 lb per week or less at 2.5 mg. The rate continues to accelerate at 7.5 mg and 10 mg. {{INTERNAL_LINK:Zepbound weight loss by dose}}

What if I cannot tolerate 5 mg?

If side effects at 5 mg are difficult, your provider can extend the 2.5 mg period and try again in 2-4 weeks. Some patients benefit from an interim step of 3.75 mg (if available through compounding). Tolerance usually develops with repeated exposure.

Will my clothes start fitting differently soon?

Most patients notice clothing changes around weeks 8-12, corresponding to 8-12% body weight loss. The face and midsection tend to change first. At week 3, visible changes are unlikely for most people.

Should I track my food intake more carefully now?

At higher doses, tracking protein intake becomes more important than tracking total calories. As appetite drops further, making sure every meal includes adequate protein prevents muscle loss. Aim for 80-100g protein daily. {{INTERNAL_LINK:nutrition on Zepbound}}
