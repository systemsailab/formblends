Living Over 300 Lbs on Wegovy

The Physical Environment

The world is not built for bodies over 300 pounds. Restaurant booths, airplane seats, office chairs, and amusement park rides all have weight and size limits. Wegovy does not fix this overnight, but as weight drops, these daily friction points begin to resolve. Many patients describe the first time they fit comfortably in an airplane seat without an extender as one of their most emotional milestones.

Shopping for Clothes

Weight loss from 300+ lbs means your clothing sizes change frequently. Buy inexpensive transitional clothes from thrift stores or discount retailers during active weight loss. Save investment purchases for when your weight stabilizes.

Relationships and Social Life

Significant weight loss changes how people treat you, and that can bring up complicated emotions. Some relationships improve; others become strained. Support from a therapist who understands the psychological dimensions of major weight loss is valuable during this transition.

Meal Planning

  • Focus on protein: 150+ grams daily from whole food sources and shakes
  • Batch cooking: Grill chicken, cook ground turkey, bake fish in bulk on weekends
  • Simple meals: Protein + vegetable + moderate carb. Repeat. Complexity is the enemy of consistency.
  • Hydration: 80 to 100+ ounces of water daily. Larger bodies need more water, and Wegovy's GI effects demand good hydration.
