Cardiovascular Protection for Limited-Mobility Patients

Joint pain patients face elevated cardiovascular risk because their pain limits physical activity, a key modifiable cardiovascular risk factor. The SELECT trial's 20% reduction in major cardiovascular events is particularly valuable for this population, where cardiovascular risk accumulates silently behind the primary complaint of joint pain.

NSAID Reduction and Kidney Protection

Chronic joint pain patients frequently rely on daily NSAIDs, which carry significant risks for kidney function, cardiovascular events, and GI bleeding. As weight loss from Wegovy reduces joint pain, many patients are able to reduce their NSAID consumption. This secondary benefit protects the kidneys and cardiovascular system, adding health value beyond the direct joint benefits .

Important Safety Information

Wegovy carries a boxed warning for thyroid C-cell tumors in rodent studies. Contraindicated with MTC or MEN2 history .

Joint pain-specific guidance:

  • Progressive activity: Increase physical activity gradually as joints tolerate more. Start with pool exercises, cycling, or walking on flat surfaces
  • Muscle preservation: Adequate protein and resistance exercise protect the muscles that stabilize joints
  • Joint-friendly movement: Avoid high-impact activities (running, jumping) until significant weight loss is achieved
  • Orthopedic follow-up: If considering joint replacement, keep your surgeon informed about your weight loss progress
  • GI side effects: Nausea (44%) is common during dose escalation but improves over time
