How Wegovy Works in Your Body

Wegovy contains semaglutide at a 2.4 mg weekly dose. It works by mimicking GLP-1, a hormone your gut produces after eating. This hormone signals your brain that you are satisfied, slows how quickly food leaves your stomach, and improves how your body processes blood sugar.

For women over 50, the mechanism is valuable because it addresses the hormonal root of increased hunger. When your estrogen-driven satiety signals have weakened, Wegovy provides an alternative pathway to feeling full and in control of your eating.

Menopause and Weight: The Challenge Wegovy Addresses

You may have heard that menopause causes weight gain. The truth is more nuanced. Menopause changes where fat is stored and how efficiently your body burns calories, while also increasing cravings and disrupting sleep. The result is a slow, steady accumulation that feels impossible to reverse.

  • Metabolic rate drops: With less estrogen and less muscle mass, your body burns 200 to 300 fewer calories per day than it did a decade ago
  • Cravings intensify: Fluctuating and declining hormones can increase carbohydrate cravings and emotional eating patterns
  • Exercise feels harder: Joint stiffness, reduced energy, and longer recovery times can make consistent exercise challenging
  • Stress eating increases: Many women over 50 are in the "sandwich generation," caring for aging parents while supporting adult children, creating chronic stress

Wegovy does not fix all of these issues, but by controlling appetite at the hormonal level, it removes the biggest barrier and lets you focus on building sustainable habits.

What the Clinical Evidence Shows

The STEP clinical trial program included women over 50 and demonstrated:

  • Average weight loss of nearly 15% of body weight over 68 weeks
  • Significant reductions in waist circumference and visceral fat
  • Improvements in blood pressure, cholesterol, and blood sugar levels
  • Reduced risk of cardiovascular events in overweight/obese adults

Protecting Your Bones on Wegovy

This is perhaps the most important consideration for women over 50. Postmenopausal women already face elevated osteoporosis risk, and weight loss can further reduce bone density.

Protective strategies:

  • Get a baseline DEXA scan before starting Wegovy
  • Take 1200 mg calcium and 1000-2000 IU vitamin D daily
  • Do weight-bearing exercises (walking, stair climbing, light impact activities) regularly
  • Include resistance training 2 to 3 times weekly
  • Avoid crash-dieting or extremely rapid weight loss
  • Follow up with DEXA scans at 12-month intervals during treatment

Common Side Effects and How to Manage Them

  • Nausea: Most common in the first 4 to 8 weeks. Eat smaller meals, avoid lying down after eating, and choose bland foods when nauseous.
  • Constipation: Increase fiber and water intake. A daily walk also helps keep things moving.
  • Fatigue: Some women feel tired during dose increases. This usually resolves within a week or two.
  • Hair thinning: Some women notice temporary hair shedding during rapid weight loss. This is related to caloric deficit rather than the medication itself and typically resolves.
  • Gallstones: Rapid weight loss increases gallstone risk. Report any sharp upper-right abdominal pain immediately.

Lifestyle Tips for Best Results

  • Protein at every meal: Aim for 25 to 30 grams per meal. Cottage cheese, Greek yogurt, chicken, fish, eggs, and lentils are excellent options.
  • Strength training: This is the single most important habit for preserving muscle and bone. Even bodyweight exercises at home make a real difference.
  • Stay hydrated: Aim for 8 to 10 glasses of water daily. Reduced food intake means less water from food sources.
  • Manage menopause symptoms: If hot flashes or night sweats are disrupting your sleep, address them. Poor sleep undermines weight loss. Talk to your physician about HRT or other options.
  • Track your progress: Use waist measurements and how clothes fit rather than relying solely on the scale. Body composition improvements may not always show up as dramatic scale changes.
