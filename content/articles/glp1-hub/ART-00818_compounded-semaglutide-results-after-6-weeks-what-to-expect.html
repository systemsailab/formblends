How long should I plan to stay on compounded semaglutide?

Most treatment plans run 12 months or longer. GLP-1 medications are most effective with continued use. Discontinuation often leads to some weight regain, so your provider will help you develop a sustainable long-term plan .

What happens if I cannot tolerate the 0.5 mg dose?

One advantage of compounded semaglutide is dosing flexibility. If 0.5 mg causes difficult side effects, your provider can prescribe an intermediate dose (like 0.375 mg) that is not available in brand-name pens. This customization is one of the key benefits of compounding.

Does compounded semaglutide need to be refrigerated?

Yes. Compounded semaglutide should be stored in the refrigerator to maintain potency. Follow the storage instructions provided by your pharmacy, and do not use medication past its expiration date .
