What dose of tirzepatide produces 80-pound weight loss?

How does 80 pounds on tirzepatide compare to gastric sleeve surgery?

Gastric sleeve patients typically lose 60 to 70% of excess body weight, which often overlaps with what tirzepatide produces. At 80 pounds, most patients have loose skin to some degree. The extent depends on age (younger patients fare better), genetics, how long the weight was carried, and how much muscle you build. Surgical body contouring is the most effective solution for significant skin laxity.

Can I stay on tirzepatide indefinitely after losing 80 pounds?

Long-term use is appropriate and often recommended. Clinical trials have studied tirzepatide for up to 72 weeks, and ongoing real-world data supports extended use.
