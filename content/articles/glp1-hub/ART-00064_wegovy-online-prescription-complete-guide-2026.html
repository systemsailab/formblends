How Online Wegovy Prescriptions Differ from Compounded Semaglutide

An important distinction that confuses many patients: brand-name Wegovy and compounded semaglutide are not the same product, even though they contain a related active ingredient.

Brand-name Wegovy is manufactured by Novo Nordisk under strict FDA oversight with validated potency, purity, and sterility. Each pre-filled pen delivers a precise, tested dose. The medication has been through extensive clinical trials (STEP program, SELECT trial) proving its safety and efficacy.

Compounded semaglutide, by contrast, is prepared by compounding pharmacies that mix their own formulations. These products are not FDA-approved, have not undergone the same clinical testing, and may use semaglutide sodium salt (a different chemical form) rather than the semaglutide base used in Wegovy. The FDA has issued multiple warnings about adverse events associated with compounded semaglutide products, including reports of incorrect dosing, contamination, and patients requiring hospitalization {{CITE:FDA compounded semaglutide adverse event reports}}.

Special Considerations for Specific Populations

Women of Childbearing Age

Your online provider should discuss contraception during the consultation if you are of childbearing potential. Wegovy must be discontinued at least 2 months before planned pregnancy due to semaglutide's long half-life. Additionally, weight loss with Wegovy can restore fertility in women who were previously anovulatory due to obesity or PCOS, meaning pregnancy can occur unexpectedly. Reliable contraception is essential during treatment if you do not wish to become pregnant {{MEDICAL_REVIEW:Wegovy fertility and contraception counseling}}.

Patients on Insulin or Sulfonylureas

If you have type 2 diabetes and take insulin or a sulfonylurea (glipizide, glyburide, glimepiride), your provider will need to adjust those medication doses when starting Wegovy to prevent hypoglycemia. This is a standard part of the prescribing process and does not disqualify you from treatment, but it does require careful coordination.

Patients with Mental Health Conditions

Your provider should ask about your mental health history during the consultation. While semaglutide has not been shown to cause depression or suicidal ideation in clinical trials, the FDA's post-marketing surveillance system continues to monitor reports. Patients with a history of eating disorders should discuss this openly, as Wegovy's appetite-suppressing effects may interact with disordered eating patterns in complex ways {{MEDICAL_REVIEW:semaglutide and mental health screening}}.

Older Adults

Patients over 65 can use Wegovy, and the STEP trials included participants up to age 75. However, older adults face additional considerations including increased risk of bone density loss during weight loss, greater vulnerability to dehydration from GI side effects, and the importance of preserving muscle mass. Your online provider may recommend DEXA scans, calcium and vitamin D supplementation, and a resistance training program as part of your treatment plan.
