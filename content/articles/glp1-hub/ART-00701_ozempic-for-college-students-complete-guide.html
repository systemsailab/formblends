How Ozempic Changes Campus Eating

Before Ozempic, a typical dining hall visit might look like: burger, fries, a bowl of pasta, two cookies, and a soda. Practical Ozempic Tips for Campus Life

Dorm Storage

Ozempic's in-use pen stays good at room temperature for 56 days. That is almost two months with no fridge needed. Keep your in-use pen in a drawer or toiletry bag at room temperature. Unused pens (if you buy ahead) need refrigeration in your mini-fridge. The pen cap protects the needle area; always replace it after use.

Injection Day

Most students pick Sunday evening. It starts the week with peak appetite control and aligns with a day that usually has a predictable routine. The injection takes 10 seconds: remove the cap, press to your stomach or thigh, hold for six seconds, done. You do not need to watch a YouTube video after the first time.

Staying Nourished

Ozempic can reduce your appetite so much that you undereat, especially during stressful weeks when you skip meals unintentionally. Set meal reminders on your phone during exam periods. Eat protein at every meal even if you are not hungry. A protein shake takes 30 seconds to make and provides 25+ grams of muscle-protecting fuel. Target 0.7 to 1.0 grams of protein per pound of body weight daily.
