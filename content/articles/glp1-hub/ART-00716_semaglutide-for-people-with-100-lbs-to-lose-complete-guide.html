Nutrition Strategy for Major Weight Loss

The Protein Rule

When losing 100 pounds, muscle preservation is your top nutritional priority. You cannot afford to lose significant lean mass because it reduces your metabolic rate and makes regain more likely. Protein targets:

  • 0.8 to 1.0 grams per pound of target body weight daily
  • If your target is 180 lbs, aim for 145 to 180 grams of protein per day
  • Spread across 4 to 5 eating occasions for optimal muscle protein synthesis

Calorie Quality Over Quantity

Semaglutide handles the quantity side by suppressing appetite. Your job is quality: lean proteins, vegetables, whole grains, healthy fats. Every calorie needs to deliver nutrients because you are eating fewer of them. Empty calories (soda, candy, chips) waste limited nutritional real estate.

Meal Prep Is Essential

Losing 100 pounds takes a year or more. You cannot eat out every meal for a year and hit your nutrition targets. Learn to batch cook simple, protein-rich meals.
