Yes. Most telehealth weight loss programs do not require long-term contracts. You will need a new consultation with your new provider, who may adjust your protocol based on their clinical assessment.

Not necessarily. If you provide your current dosing information, your new physician can evaluate whether to continue at your current dose or make adjustments.
