SkinnyRx Complaints: The Biggest Issues Patients Report

SkinnyRx complaints cluster around four main areas: unresponsive customer support, billing disputes after cancellation, shallow provider consultations, and shipping delays without communication. These are not isolated incidents. They represent recurring patterns across multiple review platforms. If you are considering SkinnyRx, understanding these complaints upfront can help you avoid the worst pitfalls or decide if a different platform is a better fit.

What Is SkinnyRx?

SkinnyRx is a budget-focused telehealth platform that prescribes compounded semaglutide and tirzepatide for weight loss. The service runs on a monthly subscription that includes a provider evaluation, medication, and shipping. It is known for low prices and fast onboarding but has accumulated complaints in several areas. {{INTERNAL_LINK:SkinnyRx review 2026}}

How It Works

You complete a health questionnaire, get a provider review, and receive compounded GLP-1 medication monthly. The subscription renews automatically unless you cancel before your billing date. No contracts, no enrollment fees.

Complaint 1: Unresponsive Customer Support

This is the most frequent SkinnyRx complaint. Patients describe:

  • Emails that go unanswered for days or weeks
  • No phone support option, limiting communication to email and portal messages
  • Generic, template-style responses that do not address the specific issue
  • Circular escalation processes where patients are asked to repeat their problem to different agents
  • Particularly slow responses for billing disputes and cancellation confirmations

For a platform that handles prescription medication, the inability to reach someone in a reasonable timeframe is a serious operational shortcoming. {{CITE:SkinnyRx customer support response time complaints}}

Complaint 2: Billing After Cancellation

The second most common complaint involves being charged after attempting to cancel:

  • Patients submit cancellation requests but are billed for the following month
  • The cancellation process is not always clear or accessible in the patient portal
  • Some patients report needing to request cancellation multiple times before it is processed
  • Refunds for post-cancellation charges are slow to process and sometimes denied

This type of billing complaint creates real trust issues. Even if the root cause is a processing delay rather than intentional overcharging, the result for the patient is the same: money taken without consent. {{CITE:SkinnyRx billing dispute records}}

Complaint 3: Shallow Clinical Interactions

Patients who expect meaningful medical oversight describe disappointment with SkinnyRx's provider model:

  • Initial evaluations feel like checkbox exercises rather than thorough medical assessments
  • Follow-up check-ins are brief, sometimes just a few questions via message
  • Questions about side effects or medication adjustments do not always get detailed responses
  • It is often unclear whether the provider reviewing your case is a physician, NP, or PA

For generally healthy patients on a straightforward GLP-1 protocol, this level of oversight may be acceptable. For patients with comorbidities, medication interactions, or side effect concerns, it is not enough. {{MEDICAL_REVIEW:adequate clinical evaluation is essential for safe GLP-1 prescribing}}

Complaint 4: Shipping Delays

While most SkinnyRx orders ship on schedule, a notable subset of patients reports:

  • Medication arriving a week or more late without explanation
  • No tracking updates during the delay
  • No proactive communication from SkinnyRx about the delay
  • Difficulty getting credits or refunds for significantly delayed shipments

Gaps in GLP-1 medication can cause side effects to return when treatment restarts, making shipping consistency more than just a convenience issue.

Complaint 5: Marketing Overpromises

A smaller but recurring complaint involves SkinnyRx's marketing:

  • Before-and-after photos that may not be representative of typical results
  • Messaging that implies rapid, dramatic weight loss
  • Understating the time commitment required for meaningful GLP-1 results

GLP-1 medications are clinically effective, but responsible marketing should set realistic expectations. Patients who sign up expecting fast, easy results are more likely to be disappointed. {{CITE:FTC guidelines on weight loss advertising}}

How to Protect Yourself

If you use SkinnyRx despite these complaints, take these steps:

  • Screenshot every step of the cancellation process
  • Cancel 7 or more days before your billing date
  • Monitor your bank statement for 60 days after canceling
  • Keep all email exchanges with customer support
  • Ask for the name and credentials of your prescribing provider
  • Request the name of the compounding pharmacy filling your prescription
  • If you are charged after canceling, file a credit card dispute promptly

Who Is SkinnyRx Best For?

SkinnyRx works best for self-sufficient, healthy patients who primarily need affordable medication access and can manage their subscription carefully. It is not suited for patients who need strong clinical support or responsive customer service.
