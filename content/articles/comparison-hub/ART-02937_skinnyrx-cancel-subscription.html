How to Cancel SkinnyRx Subscription: Complete Guide

To cancel your SkinnyRx subscription, log into your account, navigate to subscription or billing settings, and submit a cancellation request. Do this at least 7 days before your next billing date to avoid being charged for another month. What Is SkinnyRx?

SkinnyRx is a telehealth platform offering compounded GLP-1 weight loss medication on a monthly subscription. It operates month-to-month with no contract, which means you should be able to cancel at any time. In practice, however, some patients have reported friction in the cancellation process. Step-by-Step: How to Cancel

Method 1: Through Your Account Portal

  1. Log into your SkinnyRx account.
  2. Go to account settings, billing, or subscription management.
  3. Look for a cancel, pause, or manage subscription option.
  4. Follow the prompts to submit your cancellation.
  5. Screenshot the confirmation page immediately.
  6. Check for a confirmation email. If you do not receive one within 24 hours, follow up.

Method 2: By Email

  1. Send an email to SkinnyRx customer support requesting cancellation.
  2. Include your full name, the email address associated with your account, and a clear statement: "I am requesting cancellation of my SkinnyRx subscription effective immediately."
  3. Ask for written confirmation that the cancellation has been processed.
  4. Save the entire email thread.

When to Cancel

Submit your cancellation at least 7 days before your next billing date. SkinnyRx's processing times can vary, and leaving a buffer reduces the chance of being charged for another month. If your billing date is the 20th, cancel by the 13th at the latest.

Common Cancellation Problems

Patient reviews highlight several recurring issues with SkinnyRx cancellations:

  • Charged after canceling: The most frequent complaint. Patients submit a cancellation but are billed for the next month. This typically happens when the request is not processed before the billing cycle renews.
  • No confirmation received: Some patients do not receive a confirmation email after canceling, leaving them unsure whether the request went through.
  • Portal confusion: The cancellation option is not always easy to find in the account settings. Some patients report not being able to locate it and needing to email support instead.
  • Slow support response: Patients who contact support to cancel sometimes wait days for a response, during which their billing cycle may renew.
  • Refund denials: Once medication has shipped for the new billing cycle, refunds are typically denied, even if the patient intended to cancel beforehand.

How to Protect Yourself

  • Set a recurring calendar reminder 10 days before each billing date.
  • Screenshot every step of the cancellation process, including the confirmation page.
  • If canceling by email, request a read receipt.
  • Follow up within 24 hours if you do not receive confirmation.
  • Monitor your credit card or bank statement for 2 billing cycles after canceling.
  • If charged after cancellation, contact SkinnyRx with your documentation. If unresolved, dispute the charge with your credit card issuer.
  • Consider removing your payment method from the account after canceling (if the portal allows it).

Can You Pause Instead of Cancel?

Some telehealth platforms allow patients to pause their subscription for a month or two. Check your SkinnyRx account settings for a pause option. Pausing skips the next shipment and billing cycle without fully closing your account, which can be useful if you want to take a temporary break.

If no pause option exists, your only choice is to cancel entirely. You can re-enroll later, though you may need to complete a new intake assessment.

What Happens After You Cancel

  • Your subscription ends at the close of your current billing period.
  • No further medication will be shipped.
  • Any medication you have already received is yours to keep.
  • Your provider relationship ends (you will no longer have access to check-ins or messaging).
  • Your account data may be retained per SkinnyRx's privacy policy.

If you have leftover medication, consult with your physician (at SkinnyRx or elsewhere) about whether to continue using it or how to properly dispose of it.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a fee to cancel SkinnyRx?

No. There is no cancellation fee or early termination penalty.

Will I get a refund for the rest of the month?

No. SkinnyRx does not offer pro-rated refunds. Your access continues through the end of your current billing period.

Can I cancel SkinnyRx by phone?

SkinnyRx primarily supports cancellation through the portal and email. Check their website for current contact options. Email is recommended so you have written proof.

What if I am charged after I canceled?

Contact SkinnyRx customer support with proof of your cancellation (screenshots, emails). If they do not resolve the issue within a reasonable timeframe, file a dispute with your credit card company.
