Steps to Cancel Your PlushCare Subscription

  1. Log into your account. Access your PlushCare patient portal and look for subscription or account management settings
  2. Find the cancellation option. Some platforms offer direct cancellation through the portal. Others require you to email support or contact customer service
  3. Submit your cancellation request. If cancellation is not available through self-service, email or message PlushCare support with a clear request to cancel your subscription
  4. Confirm in writing. Request written confirmation that your subscription has been canceled and note the effective date
  5. Monitor your billing. Check your bank or credit card statement for the next 1 to 2 billing cycles to confirm no additional charges appear

Timing Your Cancellation

Most telehealth platforms bill on a monthly cycle. To avoid being charged for an additional month, submit your cancellation request at least a few days before your next billing date. Some platforms require cancellation within a specific window, so check the terms of service for exact timing requirements.

If you have medication currently in transit or recently shipped, cancellation typically does not affect that shipment. You keep what has already been dispatched, but no further shipments will be sent after your cancellation takes effect.

What About Refunds?

Refund policies vary widely across telehealth platforms. Once medication has been prescribed and shipped, most companies do not offer refunds for that billing period. If you are canceling due to a service issue (billing error, medication problem, or unfulfilled order), document the issue and reference it in your cancellation request.
