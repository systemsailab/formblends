What Is TrimRx?

TrimRx is a telehealth weight loss company prescribing compounded GLP-1 medications through an online subscription model. Patients complete a health intake, get a provider evaluation, and receive compounded semaglutide or tirzepatide shipped monthly. The platform does not offer brand-name GLP-1 drugs and does not work with insurance. {{INTERNAL_LINK:TrimRx review 2026}}

How It Works

  1. Complete a detailed online health questionnaire.
  2. A licensed provider reviews your information and may follow up with questions.
  3. If approved, a prescription goes to a partner compounding pharmacy.
  4. Medication, supplies, and instructions ship to your door.
  5. Monthly check-ins track your progress and allow dose adjustments.

The process is efficient. Most patients get from signup to their first shipment within about a week. The provider evaluation sits somewhere between the rubber-stamp approach of budget platforms and the thorough consultations of premium clinics.

What TrimRx Does Well

Fair pricing. TrimRx does not gouge on price, and the all-inclusive monthly fee keeps things predictable. No enrollment fees, no surprise charges, no shipping costs.

Smooth onboarding. The signup process is well designed. The health questionnaire is thorough enough to feel legitimate without being so long that it discourages completion. Provider turnaround on evaluations is quick.

Medication options. Having both semaglutide and tirzepatide gives your provider flexibility. If one medication is not working or causing too many side effects, switching to the other is possible without changing platforms.

No commitment. Month-to-month billing is genuinely flexible. You can try TrimRx for a month and walk away if it is not right for you.
