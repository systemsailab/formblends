How Your Monthly Cost May Change Over Time

Unlike fixed-rate telehealth subscriptions, Eden Health's monthly cost can fluctuate based on several factors:

Dose Escalation

As your GLP-1 dose increases through the standard escalation protocol, your medication cost may change. Higher doses of brand-name medications sometimes fall into different copay tiers. Compounded medications may also be priced differently at higher dose levels.

Insurance Changes

If your employer changes health plans during open enrollment, your Eden Health coverage and medication copays could change significantly. This is a risk unique to employer-sponsored models.

Formulary Updates

Insurance formularies change annually. A GLP-1 medication that was covered this year might move to a higher copay tier or be removed from the formulary next year.

Treatment Adjustments

If your Eden Health provider adds services (lab tests, specialist referrals, behavioral health sessions), each may carry its own copay or cost, increasing your monthly total.

Strategies to Minimize Your Monthly Eden Health Cost

  • Maximize employer benefits. If Eden Health is available through your employer, enroll during open enrollment. Even partial coverage can save hundreds per month.
  • Ask about formulary placement. Before starting treatment, ask your pharmacy benefit manager which GLP-1 medications are on the preferred tier.
