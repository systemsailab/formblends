Handling the Dosing Transition

One of the top concerns patients have about switching providers is whether their dose will change. Here is how we handle it.

In the vast majority of cases, your Form Blends physician will continue your current dose. If you are on 1.0 mg weekly semaglutide through Ro Body, you will typically start at 1.0 mg with Form Blends. The same principle applies to tirzepatide.

There are situations where your new physician may recommend an adjustment:

  • If you have been experiencing significant side effects, a brief dose reduction with gradual re-escalation may be appropriate
  • If your weight loss has stalled and your physician believes a dose increase could help
  • If switching between medication types (for example, from semaglutide to tirzepatide), dose equivalence adjustments are needed

Any changes are discussed with you before they are implemented. You are always part of the decision-making process.
