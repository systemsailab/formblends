What Is Eden Health?

Eden Health charges a flat monthly fee that covers your physician consultation, compounded medication, injection supplies, and shipping. Your subscription renews automatically on the same date each month unless you cancel before the renewal date.

Step-by-Step Cancellation Process

Option 1: Cancel Through the Patient Portal

  1. Log into your Eden Health patient portal.
  2. Navigate to your account or subscription settings.
  3. Look for a cancellation or pause option.
  4. Follow the prompts to confirm your cancellation.
  5. Screenshot the confirmation page for your records.

Option 2: Cancel by Contacting Customer Support

  1. Send an email to Eden Health's customer support requesting cancellation.
  2. Include your full name, account email address, and a clear statement that you want to cancel your subscription.
  3. Request written confirmation that your cancellation has been processed.
  4. Save the email thread as proof of your request.

Important Timing

Cancel at least 5 to 7 days before your next billing date. This gives Eden Health time to process the request and reduces the risk of being charged for another month. If your billing date is the 15th, submit your cancellation by the 8th or 9th to be safe.

What to Watch Out For

Based on patient reviews and complaints, here are the most common issues people encounter when canceling Eden Health:

  • Charges after cancellation: Some patients report being billed for a month even after submitting a cancellation request. This usually happens when the cancellation is submitted too close to the billing date or when the system does not process it in time.
  • No confirmation email: Not all patients receive immediate confirmation that their cancellation was processed. If you do not receive confirmation within 24 to 48 hours, follow up.
  • Portal limitations: Some patients report that the self-service cancellation option in the portal is not always available or easy to find, forcing them to contact support instead.
  • Refund difficulties: Once medication has shipped for the current billing cycle, refunds are generally not available. Cancel before your renewal date to avoid this situation.

How to Protect Yourself

  • Set a calendar reminder 7 days before your billing date every month.
  • Screenshot every step of the cancellation process.
  • Send a follow-up email if you do not receive confirmation within 48 hours.
  • Monitor your bank or credit card statement after canceling to verify no further charges.
  • If you are charged incorrectly, contact your credit card company to dispute the charge if Eden Health does not resolve it promptly.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a cancellation fee for Eden Health?

No. Eden Health does not charge a cancellation fee or early termination penalty. You simply stop your monthly subscription.

Will I get a refund if I cancel mid-month?

Generally, no. Eden Health does not offer pro-rated refunds for the remainder of a billing cycle. Your access continues through the end of the current paid month.

Can I cancel Eden Health by phone?

Eden Health's primary support channels are email and the patient portal. Check their website for the most current contact options. We recommend using email so you have a written record.

What happens to my leftover medication after I cancel?

Any medication you have already received is yours. No further shipments will be sent after your subscription ends. Follow your physician's guidance on whether to continue using remaining medication or dispose of it properly.
