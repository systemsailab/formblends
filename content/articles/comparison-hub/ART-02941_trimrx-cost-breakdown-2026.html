Hidden Costs You Should Know About

The sticker price is rarely the whole story. Here are costs that can catch you off guard with telehealth weight loss programs:

  • Dose escalation fees: As your dosage increases over time, the cost of medication often increases too. A program that starts at $250 per month may cost $400 or more once you reach a maintenance dose.
  • Supplies: Syringes, alcohol swabs, and sharps containers may or may not be included. Budget an extra $10 to $20 per month if they are not.
  • Cancellation fees: Some plans lock you into a commitment period. Canceling early may trigger a fee.
  • Switching medications: If your provider recommends switching from semaglutide to tirzepatide (or vice versa), the price difference can be significant.
  • Restarting after a gap: If you pause your subscription and restart later, some platforms charge a new consultation fee.
