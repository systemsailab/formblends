Who Done Works Best For

Done is a reasonable choice for patients who want convenient access to GLP-1 medications without visiting a clinic in person. It tends to work well for people who are self-motivated, comfortable managing their own health through a telehealth platform, and do not need intensive hands-on clinical support.

Patients with complex medical histories, multiple medications, or conditions that require close monitoring may benefit from a provider with more robust physician oversight and personalized follow-up care.

Who Should Look Elsewhere

If any of the following apply to you, consider comparing Done against other options:

  • You want a board-certified physician (not just a licensed provider) managing your care
  • You need both compounded and brand-name medication options
  • You value frequent, proactive follow-up consultations
  • You are on a tight budget and need the most cost-effective monthly rate
  • You have had negative experiences with telehealth billing practices before
