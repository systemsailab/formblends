How Dose Escalation Affects Monthly Cost

GLP-1 medications follow a dose escalation protocol. You start at a low dose and gradually increase over several weeks to reach your target dose. This is standard medical practice designed to reduce side effects and help your body adjust. {{CITE:GLP-1 dose escalation protocol}}

With Hers, the subscription price generally stays the same regardless of your dose level. This is different from some providers that charge more as your dose increases. However, if you switch to a different medication entirely (for example, from semaglutide to tirzepatide), your pricing may change. {{MEDICAL_REVIEW:Hers dose escalation and pricing}}

Typical Dose Timeline

  • Weeks 1-4: Starting dose. Side effects are usually mild. Cost is the same as your plan rate.
  • Weeks 5-8: First dose increase. Most patients notice appetite suppression becoming more pronounced.
  • Weeks 9-16: Continued escalation toward maintenance dose. Weight loss typically accelerates during this period.
  • Month 4+: Maintenance dosing. Your cost per month remains consistent on your chosen plan.

What Could Change Your Monthly Cost

While Hers promotes predictable pricing, a few scenarios could alter your monthly spend:

  • Plan tier changes: Upgrading from monthly to quarterly saves money. Downgrading from a longer plan is usually not possible mid-cycle.
  • Price increases at renewal: Hers reserves the right to adjust pricing. Your rate is locked within a billing cycle but may change when you renew.
  • Medication switches: If your provider recommends a different compound or medication, the cost structure may be different.
  • Optional add-ons: Hers offers supplementary wellness products that add to your monthly total if purchased.

Budgeting for Hers Treatment

Here are practical tips for managing the ongoing monthly cost of Hers treatment:

  • Set up a dedicated health spending fund. Even $50 per week set aside adds up to $200/month, enough to cover most Hers plans.
  • Use HSA or FSA dollars. If your weight loss treatment qualifies as a medical expense, pre-tax funds can reduce your effective cost by 20-35%. See our {{INTERNAL_LINK:Hers HSA FSA 2026}} guide.
  • Compare at enrollment time. Pricing is most flexible when you first sign up. Once you are in a plan, your options narrow until the next renewal.
  • Track your results monthly. If you are not seeing progress after 3-4 months, consult with your provider about adjustments before committing to another billing cycle.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the cheapest Hers weight loss plan per month?

The semi-annual (6-month) plan brings the monthly cost down to approximately $175 per month, which is the lowest rate Hers currently offers. {{VERIFY_PRICE:Hers}}

Does Hers charge extra for higher doses?

No. As of 2026, Hers maintains the same subscription price regardless of your semaglutide dose level. However, medication changes could trigger a price adjustment. {{CITE:Hers dosing pricing}}

Can I pause my Hers subscription and stop paying monthly?

Pausing may be available for certain programs. Contact Hers support to ask about pause options for your specific plan. Cancellation is always available at the end of your current billing cycle.

Is there a minimum number of months I have to pay?

The month-to-month plan has no minimum commitment beyond the current month. Quarterly and semi-annual plans require you to pay for the full period upfront.

Do Hers monthly costs go up after a promotional period?

If you signed up during a promotional offer, your cost will revert to the standard rate when the promotional period ends. Always check what the post-promotion price will be before subscribing. {{CITE:Hers promotional terms}}
