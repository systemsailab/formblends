Hims Overview

Hims is one of the most recognized names in direct-to-consumer telehealth. Originally built around men's wellness, Hims expanded into weight management with a GLP-1 program that leverages their existing infrastructure. Patients complete a health questionnaire, get matched with a licensed provider, and receive compounded semaglutide delivered to their door.

The strength of Hims lies in their technology. The mobile app provides weight tracking, educational content, and direct messaging with your care team. For people who want their weight loss program to feel integrated with the rest of their health routine, Hims makes that easy. The trade-off is that consultations are primarily asynchronous, which can slow down communication when you need quick answers about side effects or dose changes.

TrimRx Overview

TrimRx is a newer entrant in the telehealth weight loss market that competes primarily on price and simplicity. The platform strips away some of the bells and whistles that larger competitors offer and focuses on getting patients access to compounded semaglutide at competitive rates.
