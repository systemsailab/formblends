How to Cancel Your MEDVi Subscription: Step-by-Step Guide

Quick Verdict: You can cancel your MEDVi subscription at any time since there is no long-term contract. Cancel through your patient portal or by contacting customer support. The critical detail is timing: cancel before your renewal date to avoid being charged for the next month. Once an order has processed, MEDVi generally does not offer refunds. If cancellation happens after processing, a telehealth fee may apply.

Step-by-Step: How to Cancel

Option 1: Cancel Through Your Patient Portal

  1. Log in to your MEDVi account at medvi.org
  2. Navigate to your account or subscription settings
  3. Look for a cancellation or "manage subscription" option
  4. Follow the on-screen prompts to confirm cancellation
  5. Save or screenshot the confirmation page as proof of cancellation

Option 2: Cancel by Contacting Customer Support

  1. Send an email to MEDVi's customer support (check your welcome email or the MEDVi website for the current support email address)
  2. Include your full name, account email, and a clear statement that you want to cancel your subscription
  3. Request a written confirmation of cancellation
  4. Save the confirmation email for your records

Option 3: Contact Support via Phone

  1. Call MEDVi's customer support line during business hours
  2. Request cancellation and ask for a confirmation number or email
  3. Follow up with a written email restating your cancellation request to create a paper trail

When to Cancel

Timing is everything with MEDVi's cancellation process. Here is what you need to know:

Cancel before your renewal date. MEDVi bills on a monthly cycle. If you cancel before the renewal date, your subscription ends at the close of your current billing period and you will not be charged again.

If you cancel after the renewal date, your next month's payment may have already processed. Once an order processes, MEDVi's policy states that cancellations cannot be retroactively applied and refunds are generally not available. A telehealth fee may also be deducted.

Tip: If you are considering cancelling, do not wait until the last day. Process your cancellation at least a week before your renewal date to give yourself a buffer in case of any processing delays.

What Happens to Your Medication

After cancellation, you will retain any medication that has already been shipped and delivered. MEDVi will not send additional refills once your subscription is cancelled. If you have medication remaining from your last shipment, you can continue using it as prescribed until it runs out.

If you want to restart treatment later, you will need to re-enroll through MEDVi's standard onboarding process, which may include a new physician consultation.

Understanding the Refund Policy

MEDVi's refund policy is restrictive. Here are the key points:

Standard Cancellations

Once a prescription has been issued, refunds are generally not available. This means that if you received your medication and then decide to cancel, you will not receive a refund for that month's payment. Cancellation stops future charges but does not reverse past ones.

Medical Disqualification

If your physician determines you are medically disqualified from the program, MEDVi will issue a refund for the remainder of your subscription charges following the disqualification. This is the one scenario where a refund is explicitly guaranteed in the terms.

Results-Based Guarantee

MEDVi offers a guarantee that if you follow the program for at least five months without achieving weight loss, you may qualify for a refund. However:

  • The refund is reduced by a 25% consultation fee
  • "Following the program" is defined by MEDVi, and qualification criteria may be strict
  • Some patients report that this guarantee previously required only 3 months, then changed to 5 months {{FACT_CHECK: MEDVi changing results guarantee from 3 to 5 months}}

Common Cancellation Problems

Based on patient reports, here are the issues people encounter when trying to cancel:

Being Charged After Cancellation

Some patients report being charged for additional months after submitting a cancellation request. This can happen if the cancellation was not properly processed, if the request was submitted after the renewal date, or due to a billing system error. Having written proof of your cancellation request (email confirmation, portal screenshot) is essential for disputing any post-cancellation charges.

Difficulty Reaching Support

Customer support operates on business hours, and response times for cancellation requests can be slow. If you submit a cancellation by email and do not receive confirmation within 48 hours, follow up with a phone call.

Unexpected Telehealth Fees

MEDVi's terms state that a telehealth fee may be deducted if cancellation occurs after an order has been processed. The exact amount of this fee is not always clearly communicated upfront. Ask about potential fees before initiating cancellation if your renewal date has already passed.

How to Protect Yourself

  • Set a reminder: Mark your renewal date on your calendar and set a reminder for one week before
  • Use a credit card: Credit cards offer chargeback protections if you are charged after a confirmed cancellation
  • Document everything: Screenshot your cancellation confirmation, save emails, and note the date and time of any phone calls
  • Follow up: Do not assume a single email or portal action has been processed. Confirm receipt of your cancellation request
  • Monitor statements: Check your credit card or bank statement for at least two billing cycles after cancellation to ensure no further charges appear

Who Should Cancel MEDVi?

Consider cancelling if the medication is not producing results after adequate time (consult your physician about expected timelines), if side effects are unmanageable, if billing errors have become a recurring issue, or if you have found a more affordable or better-suited alternative.

Consider staying if the medication is working and you are seeing results, if your physician recommends continuing treatment, or if you are within the first few months and have not yet reached a therapeutic dose.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cancel MEDVi at any time?

Yes. MEDVi has no long-term contract. Cancel through your portal or by contacting support. The key is to cancel before your monthly renewal date to avoid being charged for the next period.

Will I get a refund when I cancel?

Generally, no. Once a prescription has been issued, MEDVi does not provide refunds for standard cancellations. The exception is medical disqualification or qualifying under the 5-month results-based guarantee (minus 25%).

How do I know my renewal date?

Check your patient portal or the original enrollment confirmation email. Your renewal date is typically one month from your last billing date.

What if I get charged after cancelling?

Contact MEDVi support with your cancellation confirmation. If the charge is not reversed within a reasonable timeframe, file a dispute with your credit card company.
