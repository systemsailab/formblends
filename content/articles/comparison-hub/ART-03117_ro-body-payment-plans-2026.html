  • You have had negative experiences with subscription services in the past

Cancellation, Refunds, and Plan Changes

Canceling Your Plan

Ro Body allows cancellation through their website or support team. Key details: {{CITE:Ro Body cancellation terms}}

  • Monthly plans: Cancel anytime before your next billing date. No charges after cancellation.
  • Quarterly plans: Cancel anytime, but the cancellation takes effect at the end of the current 3-month period. No prorated refunds for unused months.

Refund Policy

Once medication has been shipped, refunds are generally not available. Ro Body handles exceptions on a case-by-case basis, particularly for medical complications or adverse reactions.

Switching Plans

You can typically switch from monthly to quarterly (or vice versa) at the start of your next billing cycle. Contact Ro support to make the change before your renewal date.

Pausing Treatment

Ro Body may allow temporary pauses for certain patients. This is useful if you need to take a break from treatment for medical reasons or travel. Contact support to discuss options.

Auto-Renewal: What to Know

Both Ro Body plans auto-renew. Your payment method is charged automatically at the start of each new billing cycle. Here is what that means in practice:

  • Monthly plans renew every 30 days until you cancel
  • Quarterly plans renew every 90 days until you cancel
  • You will receive a reminder before renewal (check your email preferences)
  • Failed payments may result in a service pause or cancellation

Set a calendar reminder a few days before your renewal date if you want to evaluate whether to continue or make changes.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does Ro Body require a contract?

No. Both plans are subscriptions, not contracts. You can cancel at any time, though quarterly plans do not refund unused portions of the current cycle.

Can I pay for Ro Body upfront for 6 months?

Ro Body's longest billing cycle is quarterly (3 months). You would pay two quarterly cycles to cover 6 months. There is no 6-month plan option available. {{CITE:Ro Body plan options}}

What payment methods does Ro Body accept?

Major credit and debit cards, as well as HSA and FSA debit cards. PayPal and other digital wallets are generally not accepted.

Will Ro Body charge me if my consultation determines I'm not eligible?

If a Ro Body provider determines you are not a candidate for GLP-1 treatment, you will not be charged for the subscription. The evaluation is part of the signup process.

Can I use Afterpay or Klarna for Ro Body?

As of 2026, Ro Body does not appear to integrate with buy-now-pay-later services like Afterpay or Klarna. Check the Ro website for the most current payment options. {{CITE:Ro Body payment methods}}
