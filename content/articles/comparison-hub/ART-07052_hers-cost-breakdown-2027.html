Side Effects and Medical Oversight

GLP-1 medications come with real side effects that most patients experience to some degree:

  • Nausea, especially during dose escalation
  • Constipation or diarrhea
  • Fatigue and low energy in some patients
  • Injection site reactions
  • Rare but serious risks including pancreatitis and gallbladder issues
