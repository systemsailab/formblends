How BreezeMeds Works

BreezeMeds follows the standard telehealth weight loss workflow:

  • You complete a detailed online health assessment covering your medical history, current medications, allergies, and weight loss goals
  • A licensed healthcare provider reviews your information and conducts a virtual consultation
  • If medically appropriate, a prescription is issued for compounded semaglutide or tirzepatide
  • A partnered compounding pharmacy prepares and ships your medication
  • Monthly subscription renewals include provider follow-ups and continued medication supply

Medications Available Through BreezeMeds

BreezeMeds offers two primary GLP-1 medications in compounded form:

Compounded Semaglutide

The same active ingredient found in Ozempic (for diabetes) and Wegovy (for weight management). Semaglutide works by mimicking the GLP-1 hormone, which reduces appetite, slows gastric emptying, and helps regulate blood sugar. Clinical trials have shown average weight loss of 12 to 17 percent of body weight over 68 weeks. {{MEDICAL_REVIEW:Semaglutide clinical efficacy data}}

Compounded Tirzepatide

The active ingredient in Mounjaro and Zepbound. Tirzepatide is a dual GIP/GLP-1 receptor agonist that has demonstrated even greater average weight loss in clinical trials, with some patients losing over 20 percent of their body weight. It tends to cost more than semaglutide but may be more effective for certain patients. {{MEDICAL_REVIEW:Tirzepatide clinical efficacy and mechanism}}

Both medications are compounded by licensed pharmacies and administered as weekly subcutaneous injections. BreezeMeds should be able to provide information about which pharmacy compounds their medications upon request.
