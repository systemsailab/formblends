The Behavioral Coaching Factor

This is the biggest differentiator. Hims gives you the medication and lets you manage your own lifestyle changes. Noom Med wraps medication in a structured program designed to change how you think about food, exercise, and habits.

Research supports the idea that combining medication with behavioral intervention leads to better long-term outcomes. {{MEDICAL_REVIEW:medication plus behavioral intervention outcomes}} However, the question is whether Noom's specific approach works for you. Some patients love the daily structure. Others find it tedious or patronizing.

If you are self-motivated and already have a handle on your eating habits, Hims' medication-focused approach may be all you need. If you struggle with emotional eating, portion control, or maintaining consistency, Noom Med's coaching layer could make a real difference.

Long-Term Outlook

One concern with any GLP-1 program is what happens when you stop taking the medication. Studies show that patients who discontinue GLP-1 medications without making lasting lifestyle changes tend to regain a significant portion of lost weight. {{MEDICAL_REVIEW:GLP-1 discontinuation weight regain}}

Noom Med's behavioral component is designed to mitigate this risk. By building new habits alongside medication use, the idea is that patients are better prepared to maintain results long-term. Hims does not offer this structured support, though patients can certainly pursue lifestyle changes on their own or work with a nutritionist separately.

Can I use Noom's app without the medication component?

Yes. Noom offers a standalone behavioral weight loss app without the Med component. You can also use Hims for medication and Noom's standard app for coaching, though this means managing two separate subscriptions. {{CITE:Noom standalone app}}

How much time does Noom Med require each day?

Noom recommends spending 10 to 15 minutes daily on lessons and food logging. Some patients find this manageable; others find it hard to maintain over weeks and months. {{CITE:Noom daily time commitment}}

Will I regain weight if I stop GLP-1 medication?

Weight regain is common after stopping GLP-1 medications. Building sustainable eating and exercise habits while on medication can help reduce this risk. This is one reason Noom Med pairs medication with behavioral coaching. {{MEDICAL_REVIEW:post-GLP-1 weight maintenance}}

Are Noom's coaches qualified medical professionals?

Noom's behavioral coaches are trained in Noom's methodology but are not necessarily licensed clinicians. The prescribing clinician is separate from the behavioral coach. {{CITE:Noom coaching qualifications}}

Can I switch between Hims and Noom Med?
