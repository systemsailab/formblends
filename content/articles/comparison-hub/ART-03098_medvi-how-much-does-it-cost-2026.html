Detailed Breakdown: Where Your Money Goes

The Medication

The largest portion of your MEDVi payment covers the compounded medication itself. Compounding pharmacies (503B facilities) produce semaglutide or tirzepatide in injectable form, and the cost scales with the dose. A 0.25 mg semaglutide vial costs less to produce than a 1.7 mg vial. That cost difference flows directly into your monthly price.

This is why your first month is cheaper than your sixth month. You start low, titrate up, and your price follows.

The Medical Care

MEDVi includes provider consultations in the monthly fee. Your initial evaluation covers your medical history, current health status, and suitability for GLP-1 therapy. Ongoing check-ins happen through the platform, and dose adjustments are managed by your assigned provider.

The level of medical involvement is comparable to most telehealth GLP-1 platforms. You get a licensed prescriber who reviews your case, monitors your progress, and adjusts your medication as needed.
