How Hers Sets Their Pricing

Understanding how Hers arrives at these numbers can help you evaluate whether the pricing is fair. Several factors drive their cost structure:

Compounded Medication Costs

Hers uses compounded GLP-1 medications rather than brand-name products like Wegovy or Ozempic. Compounding pharmacies create custom formulations at a fraction of the brand-name cost, which is why Hers can price far below the $1,000+ monthly cost of retail semaglutide. {{CITE:compounded semaglutide cost structure}}

Telehealth Provider Fees

Licensed physicians and nurse practitioners conduct consultations and manage prescriptions. Their time is a significant portion of the subscription cost, though telehealth delivery keeps overhead much lower than brick-and-mortar clinics.

Platform and Operations

App development, customer support, shipping logistics, and regulatory compliance all factor into the final price. Hers invests heavily in branding and user experience, which is reflected in their pricing relative to less polished competitors.
