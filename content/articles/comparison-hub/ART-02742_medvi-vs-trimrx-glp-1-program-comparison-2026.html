MEDVi vs TrimRx: GLP-1 Program Comparison 2026

Bottom line: MEDVi and TrimRx are both telehealth platforms offering compounded GLP-1 medications, but they differ in clinical approach and pricing. TrimRx leans toward affordability and convenience, offering a no-frills path to compounded semaglutide and tirzepatide. MEDVi charges more but provides physician-led consultations with comprehensive metabolic lab work. For most healthy patients who want simple medication access at a lower cost, TrimRx is the practical choice. For patients who value thorough medical oversight and want lab work built into the program, MEDVi is the stronger option.

Pricing Breakdown

TrimRx undercuts MEDVi by roughly $90-$100 per month at comparable dosing levels. TrimRx plans for compounded semaglutide start around $159/month, while tirzepatide options run up to about $329. MEDVi's range of $249-$399 covers the same medication types but includes comprehensive metabolic bloodwork.

The math is straightforward. If you would otherwise skip lab work or already get it through your primary care doctor, TrimRx saves you real money every month. If you need metabolic panels and would pay $200-$400 out of pocket for them, MEDVi's higher price is partially offset by the included labs.

Neither platform accepts insurance. Both are cash-pay, with some patients using HSA or FSA funds. Neither charges enrollment or cancellation fees.

Medications Available

The medication lineups are nearly identical. Both prescribe compounded semaglutide and compounded tirzepatide sourced from licensed 503A or 503B compounding pharmacies. Neither offers brand-name GLP-1 products like Wegovy, Ozempic, or Mounjaro.

Where they differ is in medication management. MEDVi follows a more hands-on titration approach, with physicians actively guiding dose adjustments based on patient response, lab results, and side effect profiles. TrimRx provides standard titration schedules with the expectation that patients will reach out if they experience issues.

Both approaches work. MEDVi's is better for patients who want guidance at every step. TrimRx's is fine for patients who are comfortable self-managing and reaching out only when needed.

Patient Experience

Neither platform has invested heavily in its digital experience. Both offer functional portals for messaging providers, managing prescriptions, and scheduling consultations. Neither has the app polish of larger competitors like Hims or Ro.

MEDVi differentiates through its intake process and follow-up structure. The initial consultation is longer and more detailed, and follow-up appointments are scheduled proactively rather than left to the patient to initiate. This creates a sense of being actively managed rather than passively served.

TrimRx keeps things efficient. The intake is quick, the consultation is focused, and refills are handled with minimal friction. Patients who value speed and simplicity may actually prefer this. Patients who want to feel like their provider is deeply engaged in their journey will find MEDVi more satisfying.

Provider Quality and Support

MEDVi staffs all consultations with board-certified physicians. TrimRx uses a mix of MDs, NPs, and PAs. Both types of providers are clinically appropriate for GLP-1 prescribing and management.

MEDVi offers more nutritional and lifestyle guidance alongside medication management. Providers discuss dietary changes, exercise recommendations, and metabolic health strategies during follow-ups. TrimRx's support is more narrowly focused on the medication itself.

For customer support (non-clinical questions about shipping, billing, account management), both operate during business hours and are generally responsive within 24 hours.

Who Should Choose MEDVi

MEDVi is the right fit for patients who want a physician guiding their entire weight loss journey, including metabolic lab monitoring and lifestyle guidance. It is especially valuable for first-time GLP-1 patients and those with health conditions that benefit from closer monitoring.

Who Should Choose TrimRx

TrimRx suits patients who know what they want, have experience with GLP-1 medications (or are comfortable self-directing within standard protocols), and prioritize keeping monthly costs down. If you already have a doctor managing your broader health, TrimRx provides the medication without layering on services you do not need.

Frequently Asked Questions

Are TrimRx's compounded medications safe?

TrimRx sources from licensed compounding pharmacies, which are regulated at the state and federal level. The same safety considerations apply to any compounded GLP-1 medication, regardless of which platform prescribes it. The key is whether the compounding pharmacy follows proper protocols, which licensed facilities are required to do.

How long does it take to get started on each platform?

TrimRx can typically move from intake to shipped prescription within 3-5 business days. MEDVi's more thorough intake process, including lab work, can take 7-10 days before the first shipment, depending on lab turnaround time.

Can I do MEDVi's lab work through my own doctor instead?

Some MEDVi plans allow you to submit recent lab work from your own provider. Contact MEDVi directly to confirm whether your existing labs meet their requirements and whether this affects pricing.

Do either platform offer a money-back guarantee?

Neither offers a blanket money-back guarantee. Both handle refund requests on a case-by-case basis. Once medication has shipped, refunds are generally not available at either platform.
