MEDVi vs Noom Med: GLP-1 Program Comparison 2026

Bottom line: Noom Med is the better choice for patients who want GLP-1 medication paired with a structured behavior change program designed to build lasting habits. MEDVi is the better choice for patients who want a clinically focused, physician-led program with comprehensive metabolic monitoring. Noom Med's psychology-based coaching is a genuine differentiator that addresses why people regain weight after stopping medication. MEDVi's lab work and physician oversight provide deeper medical infrastructure. Both are legitimate approaches, and the right pick depends on whether your bigger concern is clinical monitoring or building sustainable habits.

Pricing Breakdown

Noom Med's pricing structure varies based on the plan you choose. The basic Noom weight loss app starts around $60/month, but Noom Med (which includes GLP-1 medication) ranges from roughly $149-$399/month depending on medication type and whether insurance covers part of the cost. MEDVi's range is $249-$399/month.

At comparable medication levels, the platforms are priced similarly. But Noom Med includes its behavior change curriculum, food logging tools, coaching, and daily lessons in that price. MEDVi includes comprehensive metabolic lab work. You are paying roughly the same amount, but the bundle is different.

One advantage Noom Med has: for patients whose insurance covers brand-name GLP-1s, Noom Med can work with those prescriptions, potentially reducing the medication portion of the cost significantly. MEDVi operates entirely outside insurance.

Medications Available

Both platforms offer compounded semaglutide and tirzepatide. Noom Med also has pathways for brand-name GLP-1 prescriptions (Wegovy, Zepbound) for patients with insurance coverage, giving it more medication flexibility.

The medication itself is only part of Noom Med's approach. Noom was built as a behavior change platform before it added GLP-1 prescribing. The company's core thesis is that medication helps patients lose weight, but behavior change helps them keep it off. This is supported by research showing that a significant percentage of patients regain weight after stopping GLP-1 medications if their habits have not changed.

MEDVi focuses more narrowly on the medical side of the equation. The assumption is that physician oversight, lab monitoring, and structured dosing will produce the best clinical outcomes. MEDVi's providers may discuss lifestyle changes, but it is not the centerpiece of the program.

Patient Experience

This is where Noom Med genuinely excels. The Noom app is one of the most developed health and wellness apps available. Daily lessons based on cognitive behavioral therapy (CBT) principles walk patients through the psychology of eating, habit formation, and emotional triggers. Integrated food logging, weight tracking, and coaching create a comprehensive daily engagement loop.

The app experience matters because GLP-1 medications are most effective when combined with lifestyle changes. Noom Med builds that structure into the platform rather than leaving it to the patient or a separate provider.

MEDVi's patient portal is utilitarian. It handles consultations, messaging, and prescription management without the engagement features that Noom provides. MEDVi's strength is clinical depth, not daily engagement.

Patients who want a platform that guides them through behavior change every day will find Noom Med more supportive. Patients who prefer a straightforward medical relationship without daily app engagement will find MEDVi less demanding.

Provider Quality and Support

MEDVi uses board-certified physicians for all consultations. Noom Med uses a mix of licensed medical providers for prescribing and a separate team of health coaches for behavioral support. The two-tier model means Noom Med patients interact with more people but may have less continuous contact with any single clinician.

Noom's health coaches are not medical providers. They are trained in Noom's CBT-based methodology and provide accountability, encouragement, and behavioral guidance. For clinical questions and medication management, patients work with Noom Med's licensed providers.

MEDVi offers a more traditional single-provider relationship where the same physician manages your medication, reviews your labs, and conducts follow-ups. This continuity can be valuable for building trust and ensuring nothing gets lost between different team members.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does Noom Med really help with long-term weight maintenance?

Noom's behavior change approach is supported by published research showing that patients who complete the program develop healthier eating habits and mindsets. Whether this fully prevents weight regain after stopping GLP-1 medication is still being studied, but the behavioral foundation Noom builds is a meaningful advantage over medication-only programs.

Can I use Noom Med without the coaching and daily lessons?

Technically yes, but you would be paying for features you are not using. If you just want medication without the behavior change program, a platform like MEDVi or another GLP-1-focused telehealth service would be more cost-effective.

Does MEDVi provide any nutritional guidance?

MEDVi providers may discuss nutritional strategies during consultations, and some plans include basic dietary guidance. However, it is not a structured, daily curriculum like Noom Med offers. The nutritional component at MEDVi is supplementary, not central.

Which platform has better results?

Neither platform publishes independently verified outcome data that allows a fair comparison. Both use the same class of medications (compounded GLP-1s), which produce similar weight loss results in clinical studies. The differentiator is what happens after and alongside medication: MEDVi monitors your metabolic health; Noom Med works on your behavior. The best results likely come from a combination of both approaches.

Can I use my Noom subscription without adding the GLP-1 medication?

Yes. Noom's original weight loss app (without medication) is still available as a standalone subscription at a lower price. Noom Med is the add-on that includes GLP-1 prescribing on top of the core behavior change program.
