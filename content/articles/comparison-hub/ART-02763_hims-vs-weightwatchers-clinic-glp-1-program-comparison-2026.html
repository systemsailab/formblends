Program Overviews

Hims

Hims is a telehealth company that has grown rapidly by making healthcare accessible through its app. Their GLP-1 program connects patients with providers who prescribe compounded semaglutide. The process is fast: fill out a health questionnaire, get a provider review, and receive medication by mail. Hims does not include nutritional coaching or community support as part of its standard weight loss subscription. {{CITE:Hims GLP-1 program}}

WeightWatchers Clinic

WeightWatchers (now often branded as WW) launched its clinical program to combine medical weight loss with its established points-based system. WeightWatchers Clinic connects patients with healthcare providers who can prescribe GLP-1 medications, including brand-name options when covered by insurance. Patients also get access to the full WW program: food tracking, recipes, community groups, and workshops. {{CITE:WeightWatchers Clinic program}}

The Lifestyle Component

The fundamental question here is whether you want medication alone or medication plus a structured lifestyle program. Research consistently shows that combining GLP-1 therapy with dietary changes and increased physical activity improves outcomes. {{MEDICAL_REVIEW:GLP-1 plus lifestyle intervention data}} But the lifestyle component only helps if you actually use it.

WeightWatchers has been helping people change their eating habits since 1963. Their Points system assigns values to foods based on nutritional content, making it easier to make better choices without strict calorie counting. The community element, including online groups and in-person workshops, provides social accountability that many people find motivating. {{CITE:WeightWatchers program history}}

Hims leaves the lifestyle piece entirely up to you. If you are already working with a dietitian, following a meal plan, or exercising regularly, you may not need or want what WW offers. You just need the prescription.

Medication and Pharmacy

Hims ships compounded semaglutide from partner pharmacies directly to patients. The process is consistent and predictable. You know what you are getting and what it costs each month.

WeightWatchers Clinic may connect patients with brand-name GLP-1 medications like Wegovy, Saxenda, or Zepbound, depending on insurance coverage. If your insurance covers these medications, the out-of-pocket cost could be lower than Hims' compounded option. If your insurance does not cover them, the brand-name price will be substantially higher. {{VERIFY_PRICE:brand-name GLP-1 medications}} WW Clinic may also offer compounded alternatives. {{CITE:WeightWatchers Clinic medication options}}

Cost Considerations

The total cost of each program depends on your situation:

  • Hims: A single monthly fee covers consultation, compounded medication, and shipping. Straightforward and predictable. {{VERIFY_PRICE:Hims}}
  • WeightWatchers Clinic: You may pay for the WW membership plus a separate medication cost. If insurance covers the medication, your net cost could be competitive. Without insurance, costs add up quickly. {{VERIFY_PRICE:WeightWatchers Clinic}}

If you already have a WW membership, adding the clinical component may feel like a natural upgrade. If you are starting from scratch, weigh whether the coaching and community are worth the extra cost.

Who Should Choose Hims?

Hims is the right pick if you want efficient, affordable access to GLP-1 medication without a bundled lifestyle program. It works well for patients who are self-directed, already have healthy eating habits, or prefer to work with their own nutritionist separately.

Who Should Choose WeightWatchers Clinic?

WeightWatchers Clinic is ideal for patients who want both medication and structured nutritional support. If you have succeeded with WW in the past, adding GLP-1 medication can accelerate results. The community aspect is a genuine advantage for people who thrive with social accountability.

Can I do WeightWatchers and Hims at the same time?

There is nothing stopping you from using Hims for medication and WW for the lifestyle program separately. However, you would be paying for two separate subscriptions, and neither platform coordinates with the other.
