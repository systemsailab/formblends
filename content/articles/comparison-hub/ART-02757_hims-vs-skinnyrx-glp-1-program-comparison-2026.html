Hims Overview

Hims started as a men's health brand focused on hair loss and erectile dysfunction before expanding into weight management. Their GLP-1 program leverages the company's established telehealth infrastructure to deliver compounded semaglutide through a subscription model. The platform provides asynchronous provider consultations, meaning you fill out a health questionnaire and a licensed clinician reviews it on their timeline.

One advantage of Hims is their polished mobile app, which bundles weight loss tracking with their other health offerings. If you already use Hims for another service, adding GLP-1 treatment feels seamless. However, some users report that the async consultation model can feel impersonal, especially when adjusting doses or managing side effects.

SkinnyRx Overview

SkinnyRx is a weight-loss-specific telehealth platform that built its entire business around GLP-1 medications. Because they specialize in this single vertical, their onboarding process and provider protocols are tuned specifically for metabolic health. SkinnyRx typically offers both compounded semaglutide and tirzepatide options, giving patients more flexibility in their treatment plan.

The platform tends to offer video consultations rather than purely async messaging, which some patients prefer when discussing injection techniques or side effect management. SkinnyRx has grown quickly in the direct-to-consumer weight loss space, though their newer market presence means fewer long-term patient reviews compared to Hims.

Treatment Options

Hims primarily offers compounded semaglutide injections along with some oral weight loss medications. Their formulary is more limited compared to some competitors, but they focus on proven options with strong clinical backing.
