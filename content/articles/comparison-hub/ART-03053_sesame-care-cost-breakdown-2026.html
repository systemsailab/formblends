What the Monthly Fee Includes

Sesame Care's monthly fee generally covers the provider consultation, prescription management, and medication supply. However, the specifics vary. Some telehealth platforms bundle everything into one monthly rate, while others charge separately for consultations, medications, and shipping.

We recommend asking these questions before enrolling:

  • Is the consultation fee included in the monthly price or charged separately?
  • Does the price change as your dose increases?
  • Are there any setup or enrollment fees?
  • What happens to pricing after the introductory period ends?

Introductory vs. Ongoing Pricing

Many telehealth weight loss platforms, including some competitors of Sesame Care, use introductory pricing to attract new patients. The first month may cost significantly less than subsequent months. This is not necessarily a bad practice, but you should calculate the cost of a full 6 to 12 months of treatment rather than focusing only on the first month.

GLP-1 weight loss treatment typically requires at least 3 to 6 months to see significant results, and many patients continue for a year or longer. Budgeting for the long term gives you a more realistic picture of what Sesame Care will actually cost.
