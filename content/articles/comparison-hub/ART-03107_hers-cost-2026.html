Factors That Affect Your Total Cost

Not everyone pays the same amount with Hers. Several variables influence your actual out-of-pocket expense.

Medication Type and Dosage

Hers primarily offers compounded semaglutide for weight loss. As your dosage increases over time (a standard part of GLP-1 treatment protocols), your cost may adjust accordingly. Starting doses are typically less expensive than maintenance doses. {{CITE:GLP-1 dose escalation pricing}}

Subscription Length

Hers offers discounts for longer commitments. A month-to-month plan costs the most per month, while a 3-month or 6-month plan reduces the per-month rate. This is the single biggest lever you have to reduce your costs.

Geographic Availability

Hers is available in most but not all U.S. states. Pricing and medication availability can vary by location due to state prescribing regulations and pharmacy licensing requirements. {{CITE:Hers state availability}}

Hidden Costs and Fees to Watch For

While Hers promotes transparent pricing, there are a few costs that can catch patients off guard:

  • Dose adjustments: If your provider changes your medication or dosage, your plan price may change at the next billing cycle.
  • Restarting treatment: If you cancel and later want to resume, you may need a new consultation and could be placed on current pricing, which may differ from your original rate.
  • Supplementary products: Hers cross-sells wellness products (supplements, skincare) during the checkout process. These are not required but can add to your total spend if you opt in.
  • No insurance billing: Hers does not bill insurance directly. You pay out of pocket and cannot submit claims to most insurers for compounded medications. {{CITE:Hers insurance policy}}

Frequently Asked Questions

Does Hers charge for the initial consultation?

No. The initial medical consultation is included in your subscription cost. You do not pay separately for the provider evaluation. {{CITE:Hers consultation pricing}}

Can I get a refund from Hers if the medication doesn't work?

Hers does not guarantee results, and refunds are generally not available once medication has shipped. If you experience side effects, your provider will adjust your treatment plan, but this does not trigger a refund. {{CITE:Hers refund policy}}

Is Hers cheaper than buying Ozempic at a pharmacy?

Typically yes, because Hers uses compounded semaglutide rather than the brand-name product. Brand-name Ozempic or Wegovy can cost over $1,000 per month without insurance. {{CITE:brand-name GLP-1 pricing}}

Does the cost of Hers go up over time?

Your cost may increase if your dosage increases or if Hers adjusts their pricing. Locking in a longer-term plan can help protect against mid-treatment price changes.

Are lab tests included in the Hers cost?

Hers does not typically require lab work for their GLP-1 program, and the subscription does not include lab testing. If your provider recommends labs, you would need to arrange and pay for those separately. {{CITE:Hers lab requirements}}
