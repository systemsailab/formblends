Best Weight Loss Programs for Men in 2026

Most weight loss programs are designed for a general audience, which in practice means they skew toward women. That is not a criticism. Women make up the majority of weight loss program enrollees. FAQ

Do GLP-1 medications work differently in men?

The medications work through the same mechanisms regardless of sex. However, men tend to have more visceral fat (abdominal fat around organs), and GLP-1s are particularly effective at reducing visceral fat. Some studies suggest men may see faster initial weight loss on GLP-1s, though long-term results are similar between sexes.

Will GLP-1 medication affect my testosterone?

Weight loss itself typically improves testosterone levels in overweight men. Excess body fat converts testosterone to estrogen through aromatization, so reducing fat mass often raises testosterone naturally. Some men report improved energy, libido, and mood as they lose weight on GLP-1 therapy.

Will I lose muscle mass on GLP-1s?

GLP-1 medications cause some lean mass loss alongside fat loss, typically in a ratio of about 25% to 40% lean mass to total weight lost. Resistance training and adequate protein intake (0.7 to 1.0 grams per pound of body weight daily) significantly reduce muscle loss. Any good weight loss program should advise men to maintain strength training during GLP-1 therapy.

Can I combine GLP-1 therapy with testosterone replacement?

Yes, under physician supervision. Some men on TRT find that adding a GLP-1 helps with the fat loss that TRT alone does not adequately address. If you are on TRT, make sure your GLP-1 prescriber knows, as it affects your overall metabolic picture. Programs like Form Blends that offer broader peptide and hormone-aware protocols can be particularly useful for these patients.

Why are men less likely to seek weight loss help?

Research points to several factors: social stigma around men admitting health struggles, marketing that positions weight loss as a women's concern, and a general tendency among men to delay healthcare. Programs with clinical, efficient approaches (like Ro Body) and male-branded platforms (like Hims) help reduce these barriers.
