Negative Review Themes

The most consistent negative theme is the lack of meaningful provider engagement. Patients describe consultations that feel like a rubber stamp rather than a real medical evaluation. Customer Support Failures

Slow and unresponsive customer support comes up repeatedly. Patients describe waiting days for responses to billing questions, cancellation requests, and shipping inquiries. Billing and Cancellation Issues

A familiar pattern in telehealth reviews: patients report being charged after they believed they had canceled, difficulty finding the cancellation option in their account, and slow processing of refund requests.
