How Monthly Costs Change Through Treatment

While your subscription rate stays fixed within a billing cycle, here is how your treatment evolves month by month and what to expect financially.

Months 1-2: Onboarding and Low-Dose Start

You complete your medical evaluation, receive your first medication shipment, and begin at the lowest dose. Your cost is the same as any other month. Some patients experience mild side effects during this phase, but the financial commitment is predictable.

Months 3-4: Dose Escalation

Your provider gradually increases your dose toward the therapeutic target. With Ro Body, your monthly price does not increase with dose escalation, which is a meaningful benefit. Some competitors charge more at higher doses.

Months 5-8: Active Weight Loss Phase

Most patients see their most significant weight loss during this period. Your monthly cost remains the same. Lab results help your provider fine-tune your treatment.

Months 9-12+: Maintenance

Once you reach your target weight or a maintenance dose, treatment continues to prevent weight regain. Some patients begin exploring lower-dose maintenance options, which may eventually reduce costs, though Ro Body's pricing is currently the same at all dose levels.
