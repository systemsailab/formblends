FAQ

Does my Hims monthly cost ever increase?

Your monthly cost does not increase with dose changes. However, Hims can adjust its base pricing at any time. If Hims raises its standard monthly rate, your next billing cycle would reflect the new price unless you are on a prepaid multi-month plan.

What happens if I miss a month?

If you cancel and then re-enroll, you may need to go through a new medical evaluation. Your pricing will be whatever Hims charges at the time of re-enrollment. Gaps in GLP-1 therapy can also require restarting the titration schedule from a lower dose.

Is $175/month a good deal for semaglutide?

For compounded semaglutide with consultations and shipping included, $175/month is competitive. It is not the absolute cheapest available, but it is well below average for the market and significantly below brand-name pricing ($1,300+/month without insurance).

Does Hims bill monthly or all at once?

Hims bills monthly by default. You are charged on the same date each month. Multi-month plans, when available, are billed upfront for the full commitment period.
