How to Cancel MEDVi

Before you cancel, take care of a few things:

Before You Cancel

  1. Download your treatment records. Your dosing history, consultation notes, and any lab results. You will need these for your next provider.
  2. Check your billing cycle. Know when your next payment is due so you can cancel before being charged for another month.
  3. Enroll with your new provider first. Start the consultation process with your replacement program before canceling MEDVi. This prevents a gap in medication.
  4. Use remaining medication. Do not throw away medication you have already paid for. Time your transition so you finish your current supply around when your new medication arrives.

Cancellation Process

Cancel through your MEDVi account dashboard or by contacting their support team directly. Request written confirmation of the cancellation and the date your subscription ends. Check your credit card statements for the next billing cycle to ensure no further charges.

If MEDVi offers to reduce your price or change your plan to keep you, consider it objectively. Sometimes a retention offer addresses your core complaint. But if the issue is clinical care quality or medication options, a discount does not fix the underlying problem.
