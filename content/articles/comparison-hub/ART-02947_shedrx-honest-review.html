The Side Effect Conversation

Any honest review of a GLP-1 program needs to address side effects candidly. These medications work, but they come with real side effects that most patients experience to some degree:

  • Nausea, especially during the first few weeks and after dose increases
  • Constipation or diarrhea
  • Fatigue and low energy in some patients
  • Reduced appetite that can sometimes feel excessive
  • Injection site reactions like redness or mild pain
  • Rare but serious risks including pancreatitis and gallbladder issues

How a platform handles side effects matters as much as how it prescribes medication. The best programs have providers who proactively adjust dosing, offer anti-nausea strategies, and are available when patients need guidance. If ShedRx's follow-up care is inconsistent, patients with significant side effects may feel stranded. {{MEDICAL_REVIEW:GLP-1 side effect profile and management strategies}}
